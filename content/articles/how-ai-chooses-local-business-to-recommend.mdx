**AI recommends the local business it can place confidently and trust — one with a
clear, consistent identity in the right area, genuine reviews, corroboration across the
local web, and content that answers the question.** Relevance, proximity, and
prominence all matter, but recognition and trust decide who gets named.

## What signals go into the decision?

A synthesis of recognition, trust, and relevance. Engines weigh a consistent business
identity ([name, address, phone](/learn/nap-consistency)), a complete [Google Business
Profile](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search), [genuine reviews and their
sentiment](/learn/local-reviews-and-ai-recommendations), [citations across reputable
directories](/learn/local-citations-and-ai-search), local mentions, proximity, and
content that matches the query. Google itself frames [local ranking as relevance,
distance, and prominence](https://support.google.com/business/answer/7091) — and AI
recommendations lean on the same signals, synthesized into a judgment about who to name.

## Does the closest business always win?

No — proximity is a factor, not the verdict. An engine recommends the business it can
place *and* trust, so a slightly farther business with a clear identity, strong reviews,
and relevant content can beat a closer one that's ambiguous or thin. [Distance matters
less than you'd expect](/learn/does-proximity-matter-in-ai-search); relevance and
prominence often outweigh it. That's good news — it means the local business that does
the work can out-recommend a closer competitor that doesn't.

## How do I become the recommended one?

Be unmistakable and trusted in your area. Make your [identity
consistent](/learn/nap-consistency) everywhere, [complete your
profile](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search), earn [genuine
reviews](/learn/local-reviews-and-ai-recommendations), build [consistent
citations](/learn/local-citations-and-ai-search), and publish [pages that answer local
questions](/learn/local-service-area-pages-for-ai). You're optimizing to be the
business an engine can confidently place and is comfortable vouching for — the whole of
[local AEO](/learn/local-aeo) in one sentence.
