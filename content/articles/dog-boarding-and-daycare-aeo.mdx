## Why is boarding and daycare your recurring-revenue engine?

Because a boarding regular and a daycare member come back again and again, filling the calendar. A
training client may graduate once, but the owner who boards every trip and brings their dog to daycare
weekly is recurring revenue that compounds — and they research with AI before they trust you. Owners
ask the assistant "dog boarding near me with webcams" or "safe daycare nearby," and the [AI answer names
only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). The facility whose pages answer the safety
and logistics questions clearly gets [cited during that research](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) — and
wins a booking that repeats for years, not a one-time job.

## What questions do owners ask about boarding?

Trust and logistics ones — each worth a clear answer on a dedicated page, because the owner is anxious
about leaving their dog.

their dog was cared for.

## How do I win boarding and daycare citations?

Give boarding and daycare their own answer-first pages — not a buried line on your homepage. Publish
your safety practices, supervision, vaccination requirements, webcam or photo updates, the daily
routine, and pricing in readable text, reinforced by reviews that mention how dogs were cared for and a
clear booking path. Own "dog boarding near me" and "dog daycare near me" with the same [near-me
discipline](/learn/near-me-dog-training-ai-search) you use for training. Most facilities bury or omit
this, so a clear, dedicated page is an easy way to get cited for recurring, calendar-filling bookings —
the repeat revenue that compounds your [growth](/learn/grow-a-dog-training-business-with-ai-search).
