**IT services companies and MSPs get found by AI search when their site is crawlable, answers the real
questions buyers ask, and is backed by stated certifications and genuine reviews.** The AI names only
a few firms, so the MSP that clears all three is the one recommended instead of a resold lead.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When a business asks for IT help, the engine pulls the firms it can
read, judges which best answer the question and look trustworthy, and [names just a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Each step is a filter: if a crawler can't fetch your
page, you're out before ranking; if your page doesn't answer the pricing or scope question, you're out
at ranking; if your trust signals are thin, a competitor gets named. Being found means clearing every
filter.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a buyer — apply, in order.

## Why can't businesses find my MSP?

Almost always one specific broken gate — not bad luck. Your site is thin or slow so [crawlers see
little](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), your pages dodge the pricing and scope questions, or
your reviews and listings are inconsistent. The fix is diagnostic: find the *highest* broken gate and
repair it first, because a great reputation can't rescue a site bots can't read, and a fast site can't
rescue a page that never answers the question. This is the same
[why-isn't-my-business-showing-up](/learn/why-isnt-my-local-business-showing-up-in-ai) logic, applied
to the industry.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking proves Google can read and rank you — a good sign for the first gate — but AI
citation also needs an [extractable, answer-first page](/learn/answer-first-sentence) and trusted
signals like certifications and reviews. An MSP can rank yet be absent from AI answers if the answer is
buried or trust is thin. The overlap is real but not total: clear all three gates and you get [found in
both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
