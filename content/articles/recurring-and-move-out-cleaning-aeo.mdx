## Why focus AEO on recurring and move-out?

Because they're the best revenue you can win. A recurring plan is predictable income every week or two that
compounds for years as your book grows; a move-out or deep clean is a high-ticket one-time job that often
becomes a recurring client. Both are decisions a client [researches with AI before
booking](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — pricing, scope, guarantees — so the service whose pages
answer those questions clearly [gets cited during the research](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) and wins the
highest-value inquiries.

## What questions do these clients ask?

Specific and high-intent — each worth a clear, dedicated page.

## How do I win these citations?

Give recurring and move-out cleaning each its own answer-first page — not a buried mention. Publish your
pricing model, what's included, frequency discounts, deposit guarantees, and turnaround in readable text,
with a clear path to book. Most maid services either omit this or hide it inside a quote widget, so a
clear, dedicated page is an easy way to get cited for the highest-value jobs — the recurring plans and
high-ticket cleans that compound your [growth](/learn/grow-a-maid-service-with-ai-search).
