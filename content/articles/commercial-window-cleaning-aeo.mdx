## How is commercial window cleaning AEO different?

The intent is a researched B2B decision, not a single home. A property manager or store owner evaluates
carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before they call. They're researching scope,
frequency, access methods, and whether you can clean around business hours. Because [AEO works for
B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the company whose pages answer those questions clearly
gets [cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a recurring account
worth far more than a single residential job.

## How do I win commercial citations?

Give commercial window cleaning its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage. Publish
your scope, frequency options, access capability, insurance, and scheduling in readable text, with a
clear inquiry path. Most window companies either omit this or hide it, so a clear, dedicated page is an
easy way to get cited for valuable, recurring commercial accounts — the kind that anchor a route and
compound your [growth](/learn/grow-a-window-gutter-business-with-ai-search).
