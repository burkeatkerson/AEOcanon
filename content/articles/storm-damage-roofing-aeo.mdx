## What questions surge after a storm?

The urgent, high-anxiety ones — and they're the most valuable queries in roofing. Right
after a storm, homeowners ask whether the damage is covered, how to file a claim, how to
document it, what to do before the adjuster arrives, how fast you can inspect, and whether
you handle the paperwork. Each is a homeowner mid-crisis looking for an expert to trust —
exactly the [moment a citation wins the relationship](/learn/roofing-questions-customers-ask-ai),
and the [Alignment pillar](/pillars/alignment) at its highest stakes.

## How do I prepare for storm season?

Build the content while it's calm, so it's ready when the surge hits.

This is the [freshness and preparation](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo)
discipline applied to roofing — one durable storm page, refreshed and ready each season,
beats scrambling to publish after the hail falls.

## Should I really explain the insurance process?

Yes — it's the highest-trust move you can make. A clear, honest explanation of the claim
process is exactly what an anxious homeowner searches for, so answering it earns trust and
citations at the decisive moment. It positions you as the helpful expert who handles the
hard part — which is precisely the roofer a homeowner wants to call, and the
[Credibility](/pillars/credibility) and [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge a generic
aggregator can't match. Be the calm, clear answer in a stressful moment and you win the
job.
