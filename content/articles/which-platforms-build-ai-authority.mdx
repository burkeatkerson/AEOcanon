**The platforms that build AI authority are the ones engines actually cite — led by
Reddit, with YouTube, Wikipedia, and established industry publications also prominent,
though the exact mix varies by topic.** Concentrate genuine participation where
engines pull citations in your niche rather than spreading across every platform.

## Which platforms carry the most weight?

The ones engines lean on. [Reddit is the single most-cited domain across AI
answers](/learn/reddit-ai-citations) ([Profound's analysis](https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/the-data-on-reddit-and-ai-search) found it the most-cited domain in AI search), and YouTube, Wikipedia, and respected industry
publications also show up heavily — so genuine presence on those surfaces builds the
off-site [authority](/pillars/authority) AEO depends on. They matter because engines
treat them as trusted corroboration, turning real participation there into a citation
signal rather than just an audience — consistent with [Ahrefs' finding that web mentions correlate with AI brand visibility far more strongly than backlinks](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-overview-brand-correlation/).

## Why does Reddit stand out?

Because it's cited far more than almost anything else. Engines pull from Reddit
constantly, so helpful, authentic participation in relevant subreddits can earn both
audience and the corroboration AEO needs. The catch is authenticity — both the
communities and the engines reward genuine contribution and discount promotion, so
the play is to be a useful participant, not a marketer dropping links.

## Should I be everywhere?

No — concentrate. Spreading thin across every platform serves none of them well, while
depth on the few surfaces engines cite in your niche compounds. Find those surfaces by
[testing the engines](/learn/track-competitor-ai-citations): ask your priority
questions and note which platforms and domains get cited in the answers. Those are
where authority lives for your topic — go deep where the citations already come from,
the same focus that [building authority from scratch](/learn/build-ai-authority-from-scratch)
demands.
