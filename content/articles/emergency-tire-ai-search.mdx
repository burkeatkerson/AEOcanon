## Why is urgent intent the highest-value tire search?

Because it converts on the spot. When a tire blows or goes flat, the driver isn't comparison-shopping —
they ask an assistant, get an answer, and drive to the first credible name. There's no page two. Pew
Research [found people click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the decision. For a tire shop, owning that moment fills the bays — and a flat fixed
today often becomes the customer's shop for their next full set.

## Why do hours decide the urgent sale?

Because 'open now near me' is a placement question, and the engine answers it from your hours. If your
hours conflict between your site and your Google profile, the engine can't confidently say you're
available *here, now* — so it names a competitor it's sure about. Keep hours accurate and identical
everywhere, state walk-in and same-day availability plainly, and you remove the most common reason a tire
shop loses the urgent sale. This is the same [local certainty](/learn/local-aeo-for-tire-shops) that wins
every near-me query, sharpened by the clock.
