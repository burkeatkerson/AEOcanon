## Is AEO a real job title?

Increasingly, but not universally. Today most organizations fold AEO into SEO, content,
or AI-marketing roles rather than hiring a dedicated specialist — though that's shifting
as AI search grows. The practical implication: treat AEO as a skill that strengthens
your candidacy for those roles *now*, and position yourself for the standalone
specializations emerging over time. The honest picture of demand and pay is in [the AEO
job market](/learn/aeo-job-market-salaries).

## Should you specialize or stay general?

Both, in order. Be literate across the [eight pillars](/learn/aeo-canon), then specialize
where you have an edge — a domain (local, ecommerce) or a discipline (technical, content,
measurement). The most valuable people connect content, technical, and measurement; the
most *hireable for a specific need* go deep in one domain. The [Specialist
courses](/courses) are built for exactly that depth.
