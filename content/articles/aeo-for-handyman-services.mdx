## Why does AEO matter so much for a handyman?

Because a homeowner with a fix in mind wants one reliable person, fast — and the answer is the new
front door. Someone with a to-do list asks an assistant before they call anyone, and the [AI answer
names only two or three sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of links. Pew
Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes who they trust before you ever hear from them. Being the cited handyman is the new
equivalent of being the name a neighbor recommends.

## How do handyman services get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most handymen have a thin, one-page site and a reputation that's inconsistent across listings — so they
fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage
work, and it's the same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast, locally-trusted site
that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering questions
[directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service pages for
extraction](/learn/handyman-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/handyman-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local handymen have the advantage?

In specificity and proof. AI names only a few sources and citations spread thin, so a trusted local
handyman who answers "what does a handyman charge in [city]" or "who mounts TVs near me" better than a
national app can take the recommendation. The app is generic and faceless; you're specific, local,
insured, and real, with before-and-after jobs to show. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality)
and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-handyman-services) edge a big aggregator can't easily copy.
