car key, or rekey — by being crawlable, answering the real cost-and-area questions first, and
earning local trust through reviews and a verifiable license.** The reward is the urgent call that
used to go to a directory or a scam operator.

## Why does AEO matter so much for locksmiths?

Because lockouts are urgent, trust-sensitive, and decided on the first credible answer. When someone
is locked out, they ask an assistant and act immediately — and the [AI answer names only two or three
sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of links. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so when someone's stranded the answer *is* the front door. For a trade plagued by fake listings,
being the cited, genuinely local locksmith is the modern version of being the name people trust.

## How do locksmiths get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a person — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most locksmiths have a slow site and a reputation that's inconsistent across listings — so they fail
gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage
work, and it's the same foundation that [ranks you in
Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast, locally-trusted site
that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering questions
[directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service pages for
extraction](/learn/locksmith-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/locksmith-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do real local locksmiths have the advantage?

In authenticity and trust. AI names only a few sources and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a real, licensed, genuinely reviewed local locksmith
can take the recommendation from the fake-listing operators that flood the directories. They're
generic and faceless — and increasingly distrusted; you're specific, local, licensed, and real.
That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-locksmiths) edge an aggregator can't copy, and a verifiable license
is proof a scam listing simply can't show.
