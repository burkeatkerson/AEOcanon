## Why is high-intent the most valuable remodeling search?

Because it's the moment money is about to move, and the assistant frames the whole decision. A
homeowner ready to remodel researches cost, timeline, and design before they contact anyone — and the
[AI answer names only a few sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found
people click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist before you ever hear from them. The remodeler cited at this
stage is the one the homeowner trusts enough to call first.

## Why does answering cost honestly matter so much?

Because cost is the first thing a homeowner researches and the question most remodeler sites dodge.
A clear, honest cost range — with the factors that move it — earns trust and citations precisely when
intent is highest. Dodging it ("every renovation is unique, call us") sends the homeowner to a
competitor or a lead-gen platform that actually answered. Being the remodeler who explains the money
honestly is the [Credibility](/pillars/credibility) and [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge that
gets you cited and called — map every one of these questions to a page in your
[questions library](/learn/remodeling-questions-customers-ask-ai).
