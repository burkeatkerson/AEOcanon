index, so being crawlable by [BingBot](/glossary/bingbot), indexed in Bing, answer-first, and
authoritative is the path.** The bonus: the same Bing visibility also feeds
ChatGPT's search, so this is some of the highest-leverage work in AEO.

## How does Copilot decide what to cite?

Copilot decides what to cite by [retrieving](/glossary/retrieval) from **Bing's search index**, [reranking](/glossary/reranking)
results for the query, and linking the sources of the passages it uses — the same
[retrieve-rerank-cite pipeline](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) as other
engines, but over Bing rather than Google or a proprietary index. Copilot is
Microsoft's assistant across Windows, Edge, Microsoft 365, and the web (it evolved
from Bing Chat), and its web grounding means the practical question is simply: *are
you visible in Bing for this query?* If Bing can't find you, Copilot can't cite you.

## Why is Bing visibility unusually high-leverage?

Bing visibility is unusually high-leverage because **two** major AI surfaces are
grounded in Bing's index: Copilot and [ChatGPT's
search](/learn/how-to-get-cited-by-chatgpt). Bing's slice of classic search is
small, which leads many teams to ignore it — but that's a mistake in an AEO world,
because the same Bing presence that earns a Copilot citation also helps you enter
ChatGPT's retrieval candidate set. One investment, two of the largest AI
assistants. That asymmetry is exactly the kind of thing per-engine measurement
surfaces and a blended average hides.

## How is Copilot different from Google's AI surfaces?

Copilot differs from Google's AI surfaces in the index it retrieves from: Bing, not
Google. A page can rank well in Google and be weak in Bing (or vice versa), so
Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, and Gemini visibility does **not** automatically carry
to Copilot. Confirm you're crawlable and indexed in Bing specifically, and measure
Copilot on its own — engines overlap only ~11%
([Profound](/learn/per-engine-vs-blended-measurement)), and the Bing-vs-Google split
is a big reason why.
