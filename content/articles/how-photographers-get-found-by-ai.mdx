**Photographers get found by AI search when their packages and pricing are readable as real text, they
answer the questions clients ask, and they're backed by accurate listings and genuine reviews.** The AI
names only a few businesses, so the photographer that clears all three is the one recommended.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When someone asks who can shoot a wedding or session, the engine pulls the
businesses it can read, judges which best fit the request and look well-reviewed, and [names just a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Each step is a filter: if the crawler can't read your packages
because the page is all images, you're out before ranking; if your service area is unclear, you're dropped
from 'near me'; if your reviews are thin, a competitor gets named. Being found means clearing every filter —
and for photographers, the readable packages and pricing are the one most often missed.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a client — apply, in order.

## Why can't clients find my photography business?

Almost always one specific broken gate — and for photographers it's usually the wordless gallery. If your
site is [beautiful photos with no readable text the crawler can parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site),
the engine doesn't know what you offer, so it can't recommend you for "wedding photographer near me" or
"family photographer for fall photos." Other common breaks: an unclear service area, or thin reviews. The fix
is diagnostic — find the *highest* broken gate and repair it first. For most photographers, putting packages,
pricing, and answers in plain text alongside the images is the single highest-leverage move.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking and a Google listing help, but AI citation also needs your [packages and answers in
readable text](/learn/photographer-service-pages-for-ai) and genuine reviews. A photographer can show up on a
map yet be absent from "best wedding photographer near me" answers if the engine can't read the packages or
the reviews are thin. Clear all three gates and you get [found in
both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
