## Why does seasonality matter for tattoo studios?

Because demand surges in predictable waves, and the cited studio catches them. Friday the 13th brings a
flash-day rush; summer brings sleeve-and-vacation season and convention travel; the holidays bring
gift-certificate buyers. The studio whose answer is already published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited
the moment the surge hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors are still
updating their site. It's the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a demand calendar, and
the difference between a packed flash day and a quiet one.

## How do I prepare for each surge?

Build the content while it's calm, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable evergreen page per theme. A persistent 'Friday the 13th flash day' or 'tattoo
gift certificates' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the date
and details each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page that starts from zero and splits your
signals. Keep one strong page per recurring surge, sharpen it before each one, and link it to your
[style and pricing pages](/learn/tattoo-studio-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and
ready, beats scrambling when the flash-day rush hits.
