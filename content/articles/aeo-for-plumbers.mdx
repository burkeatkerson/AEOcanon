## Why does AEO matter so much for plumbers?

Because plumbing is urgent, local, and decided on the first credible answer. When a
customer's pipe bursts, they ask an assistant and act immediately — and the
[AI answer names only two or three sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations),
not a page of links. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus
15% without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so for an emergency trade the answer *is* the front door. Being the cited plumber is
the new equivalent of being the first call.

## How do plumbers get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a customer — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most plumbers have a site that's slow and a reputation that's inconsistent across
listings — so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened.
Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that
[ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast,
locally-trusted site that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering
questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service pages for extraction](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening
the [reviews and listings](/learn/plumber-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer
engines lean on. You don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local plumbers have the advantage?

In specificity. AI names only a few sources and
[citations spread thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a focused local plumber
who answers "how much to replace a water heater in [city]" better than a national
directory can take the recommendation. The directory is generic; you're specific,
local, and real. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and
[local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-plumbers) edge a big aggregator can't easily
copy.
