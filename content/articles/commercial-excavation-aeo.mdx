## How is commercial excavation AEO different?

The intent is research and risk, not a homeowner's lot. A builder, GC, or developer evaluates carefully
— and asks the assistant detailed questions long before contacting anyone. They're researching site-
development experience, bonding capacity, fleet size, and whether you can deliver mass grading on a
deadline without an erosion-control violation. Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b)
exactly here, the excavator whose pages answer those questions expertly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and lands on the shortlist before a single call.
And commercial site packages are large, repeatable relationships, so each developer or GC won is worth
far more than a single residential dig.

## What content wins commercial citations?

Depth backed by proof. Commercial decisions carry higher stakes and longer research cycles, so your
pages should demonstrate genuine capacity — fleet and crew size, bonding capacity, safety and
erosion-control record, certifications, and clearly scoped capabilities — in thorough,
[answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence) pages. The fundamentals are the same as residential: be
readable, answer the real question, be trusted. Commercial just rewards [depth and
credibility](/pillars/credibility) more heavily, because the developer is betting their schedule and
budget on the dirt crew getting the site right. Be the page that proves you're the safe, high-capacity
expert, and you win the shortlist — and the repeat relationship behind it.
