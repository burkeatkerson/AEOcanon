## Why is opening season the biggest AEO lever?

Because most owners choose their pool service for the whole summer in spring, and the cited company
catches that wave. Late winter and spring bring a surge of "pool opening near me" and "weekly pool
service" searches; summer brings peak maintenance and green-pool emergencies; fall brings closings. The
company whose answer is already published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the surge
hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors scramble. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a pool calendar, and the single biggest chance to fill your
route.

## How do I prepare for each season?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'pool opening' or 'weekly pool service
plans' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the dates and pricing
each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your
signals. Keep one strong page per season, sharpen it before each surge, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/pool-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling when
the rush hits.
