## What's the growth model?

A flywheel, not a faucet. Each great sale earns a review and a lifelong customer — milestones and gifts
recur for years; [readable collections](/learn/jeweler-product-pages-for-ai) and genuine reviews earn
citations for "best jeweler near me" and "custom engagement rings"; those bring high-value buyers; and the
margin you keep — instead of paying a chain's ad rates — funds better inventory and craft. The chain model
is the opposite: a big ad budget you can't match and a customer that belongs to them. AEO builds the
[compounding asset](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) instead — loyal buyers who come to you directly, at full
margin.

## What's the fastest place to start?

Fix the [readable collections, pricing, and services](/learn/jeweler-product-pages-for-ai) first — it's
the highest-leverage gap — then make hours and listings accurate so you win [near-me
searches](/learn/local-aeo-for-jewelers), and build a steady flow of [genuine
reviews](/learn/jeweler-reviews-ai-recommendations). Those win citations within weeks. Then compound it:
turn every happy buyer into a lifelong customer (milestones recur), and add [custom-design and repair
pages](/learn/custom-and-repair-jewelry-aeo) for the high-margin work chains can't match.

## Why are chains such a hard competitor — and how do you beat them?

Because they win on ad spend, not trust. National chains and online retailers have budgets you can't
match in paid search, and the customer often belongs to them. But AI names only a few stores by relevance
and trust, *not* ad spend — so a real local jeweler with readable collections, genuine custom-design
expertise, and reviews that name beautiful rings and reliable repairs can win the citation a chain can't
buy. And that buyer becomes a lifelong customer who comes back for the anniversary band, the repair, the
next milestone. As your cited, direct, repeat base grows, your margin improves — and because AI names only
a few stores, a real, well-reviewed local jeweler can win recommendations a faceless chain
[can't replicate](/learn/how-to-make-content-ai-cant-ignore).
