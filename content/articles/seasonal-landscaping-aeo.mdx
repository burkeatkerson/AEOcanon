## Why is the spring rush the biggest AEO lever?

Because most homeowners choose their lawn care company for the whole season in early spring, and the
cited company catches that wave. Late winter and spring bring a surge of "spring cleanup near me" and
"lawn mowing service signup" searches; fall brings leaf removal and aeration; winter brings snow. The
company whose answer is already published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the surge
hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors scramble. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a seasonal calendar, and the single biggest chance to fill your
route.

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'spring cleanup' or 'lawn care plans'
page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the dates and pricing each
year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your signals.
Keep one strong page per season, sharpen it before each surge, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/landscaper-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling
when the rush hits.
