## How do I know my plumbing site needs a rebuild?

Test what an AI crawler actually sees. [Fetch a page with JavaScript
off](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site) — if the content isn't there, crawlers see
an empty shell and you're invisible before the contest starts. Check your load speed
too, against [Core Web Vitals](https://web.dev/articles/vitals). The common warning signs for trade sites: content [rendered only in the
browser](/learn/javascript-breaks-ai-citation), [page-builder
bloat](/learn/is-wordpress-good-for-aeo) that slows everything down, no individual
[service pages](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai), and inconsistent or missing
[structured data](/learn/plumber-schema-markup). Any of those caps your AEO no matter
how good your work is.

## Can't I just add content to the site I have?

Only if the foundation is sound. Adding [answer-first
content](/learn/answer-first-sentence) to a fast, crawlable site works beautifully. But
adding it to a slow, client-rendered, or poorly-structured site is building on sand —
the engine still can't read or trust the result, so the content can't earn citations it
never reaches. The [Access pillar](/pillars/access) is a precondition, not an
optimization: fix the foundation first, then layer the content.

## What does an AEO rebuild actually include?

The whole access-and-extractability foundation in one pass: a fast, server-rendered
site crawlers can read; a dedicated [answer-first page per
service](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai) and key area; correct
[LocalBusiness/Plumber and service schema](/learn/plumber-schema-markup); a cleaned-up
[Google Business Profile](/learn/plumber-google-business-profile-ai) and consistent
listings; and a structure built for ongoing content. That's a large project — and it's
[included free in our done-for-you program](/pricing) (a $12,000 build at no cost),
because we'd rather start you on a foundation that can actually be cited than optimize
one that can't.

## Then what?

Then the content compounds on a foundation that works. With a crawlable, well-structured
site in place, the [service pages](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai),
[local signals](/learn/local-aeo-for-plumbers), and [monthly
answers](/learn/plumbing-questions-customers-ask-ai) start earning citations instead of
fighting the foundation. The rebuild isn't the finish line — it's the starting line that
makes everything in [AEO for plumbers](/learn/aeo-for-plumbers) possible.
