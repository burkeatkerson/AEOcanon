**A boutique needs a website rebuild for AEO when the site is all lookbook images with no readable text,
slow, or built only in a third-party shopping widget — because the engine can only recommend what it can
read.** The rebuild puts your products, styles, sizing, and policies in readable text everything else
depends on.

## Why are your products the binding constraint?

Because [access is the first gate](/pillars/access), and for a boutique the all-image site is where you
most often fail it. Shoppers search for "plus-size dresses near me" or "where to buy sustainable denim,"
but if your products and sizing live only in [lookbook photos or a widget the crawler can't
read](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), the engine doesn't know what you sell or who you fit, so
it can't recommend you. Add a slow build, and even your basics are invisible. That's not a content
problem you can write around; it's a foundation problem.

## How do I tell if my site is hurting me?

Run two quick tests, and look for the structural gaps.

    { title: "The policy test", body: "Are your sizing, shipping, and return policies readable text on the page, or buried in the checkout flow? Engines need them to answer shopper questions." },

If your products are photo-only, your sizing and policies are widget-trapped, or the page is slow, the
site is working against you. A fast site with [readable products and
policies](/learn/boutique-product-pages-for-ai) and [clean schema](/learn/boutique-schema-markup) is
what makes everything else possible.

## Can't I just keep my shopping widget?

Keep the widget for checkout — but you also need your products, styles, sizing, and policies in readable
text on the page. Because the widget is usually invisible to crawlers, relying on it alone hides exactly
the offerings shoppers search for. Add readable text alongside it, get the access layer right —
[server-rendered](/learn/server-side-vs-client-side-rendering), fast, with readable products — and the
rest of your boutique AEO finally has something to build on.
