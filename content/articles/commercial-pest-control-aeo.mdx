## How is commercial pest control AEO different?

The intent is research and compliance, not panic. Where a homeowner with bed bugs acts on the
first answer, a restaurant owner or facility manager evaluates carefully — and asks the
assistant detailed questions long before contacting anyone. They're researching health-code
requirements, documentation practices, and whether you've handled their kind of facility.
Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the company whose
pages answer those questions expertly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and lands on the shortlist before a single
call.

## What content wins commercial citations?

Depth backed by proof. Commercial decisions carry compliance stakes and longer research
cycles, so your pages should demonstrate genuine expertise — code knowledge, documentation
and reporting practices, certifications, and clearly scoped programs — in thorough,
[answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence) pages. The fundamentals are the same as
residential: be readable, answer the real question, be trusted. Commercial just rewards
[depth and credibility](/pillars/credibility) more heavily, because the buyer is betting their
health inspection and reputation on getting the choice right. Be the page that proves you're
the safe expert, and you shape the shortlist.
