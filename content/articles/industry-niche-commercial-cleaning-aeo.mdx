## Why does a specialty win in AI search?

Because specificity beats generality, and AI names the company that best fits the exact question. When a
manager asks "medical office cleaning company that follows infection-control protocols" or "who handles
post-construction cleanup," the engine looks for a company that clearly handles that sector's
particulars — and a generalist page that says "we clean all commercial spaces" matches nothing in
particular. A focused specialty is the [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge a company can own, and
exactly the kind of specific, expert content [citations spread thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands)
reward. It's also where the money is — specialized work carries higher rates and tighter competition.

## How do I win specialty citations?

Give each sector you serve its own answer-first page covering its specific standards, compliance, and
protocols — not a buried line on a generalist page. Show you understand their infection control, their
OSHA requirements, their ISO class, reinforced by [reviews from that
sector](/learn/commercial-cleaning-reviews-ai-recommendations) and a clear path to a walkthrough. A
dedicated 'medical office cleaning' or 'post-construction cleanup' page gets cited for exactly that
query, signals real expertise, and lets you [win premium contracts and
grow](/learn/grow-a-commercial-cleaning-business-with-ai-search) — because specialists command higher
rates and longer commitments than generalists. Map each specialty to the [high-intent
searches](/learn/high-intent-commercial-cleaning-ai-search) that lead to a bid.
