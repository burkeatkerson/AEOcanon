## What query patterns do real estate clients use?

Real estate clients query AI across the journey: **"best realtor in [area]"**,
**"is [neighborhood] a good place to live"**, **"how much is my home worth"**, **"how
does [buying/selling] work"**, **"[area] housing market"**, and **"schools in
[neighborhood]"**. Agent-finding queries reward a reviewed, recognized local
presence; neighborhood and process queries reward genuine, current, locally-specific
content with real [topical authority](/glossary/topical-authority). Map the real questions buyers and sellers ask, and answer each clearly —
[alignment](/pillars/alignment) for a local, high-consideration purchase, the way an
[answer engine](/glossary/answer-engine) expects.
