win.

## Why does AEO matter so much for painters?

Because painting is a researched, money-on-the-line decision made on the first credible answer. A
homeowner planning to paint researches cost and timing before they call anyone — and the [AI answer
names only two or three contractors](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings.
Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited painter is the new equivalent of being the
contractor a neighbor recommends — without bidding against everyone who bought the same lead.

## How do painters get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most painters have a slow gallery site and inconsistent listings — so they fail gates one and three
without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same
foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering homeowner questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service pages for extraction](/learn/painter-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/painter-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose between
them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small painters have the advantage?

In specificity and proof. AI names only a few contractors and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a small local painter who answers "how much to paint a
3-bedroom interior in [city]" and shows real work can win the recommendation over a faceless directory.
That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-painters) edge
an aggregator can't copy — and you skip the price war over shared leads.
