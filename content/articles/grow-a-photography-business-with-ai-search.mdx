## What's the growth model?

A flywheel, not a faucet. Each great shoot earns a review, a referral, and a repeat client; [readable
packages](/learn/photographer-service-pages-for-ai) and genuine reviews earn citations for 'wedding
photographer near me' and 'family photographer nearby'; those bring direct inquiries; and the lead fees you
save fund better gear and your craft — which create more great shoots. The marketplace model is the opposite:
a faucet you pay a fee to keep running, that resells your lead and stops the moment you stop. AEO builds the
[compounding asset](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) instead — loyal clients who inquire direct.

## What's the fastest place to start?

Fix the [readable packages and pricing](/learn/photographer-service-pages-for-ai) first — it's the
highest-leverage gap, because most photographer sites are image-only — then make your service area and
listings accurate so you win [near-me and service-area](/learn/local-aeo-for-photographers) queries, and build
a steady flow of [genuine reviews](/learn/photographer-reviews-ai-recommendations). Those win citations within
weeks. Then compound it: turn every happy client into a review, a referral, and repeat work, and add
[wedding](/learn/wedding-photography-ai-search) and [portrait and
commercial](/learn/portrait-and-commercial-photography-aeo) pages for high-value, plannable bookings. Small
habits, compounding into an owned pipeline of direct demand.

## How does this lower my cost to win a booking?

By replacing per-lead fees with assets that keep paying. A marketplace inquiry costs a fee every single time
and is resold to four competitors; an earned citation, a recurring commercial client, or a word-of-mouth
referral costs mostly upfront and brings bookings for years. As your owned, direct demand grows, your cost per
booking falls and your calendar fills — and because AI names only a few businesses, a real, well-reviewed local
photographer can win recommendations a faceless marketplace profile
[can't replicate](/learn/how-to-make-content-ai-cant-ignore).
