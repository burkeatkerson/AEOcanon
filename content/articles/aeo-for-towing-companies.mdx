## Why does AEO matter so much for towing?

Because roadside trouble is the most urgent search there is, decided on the first credible answer. When a
driver is stranded, they ask an assistant and call immediately — and the [AI answer names only one or two
companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings. Pew Research [found people
clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the call. Being the cited company is the new equivalent of being the tow truck everyone
knows to call — and it pays full price, not a dispatched fraction.

## How do towing companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a driver — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most tow companies fail gates one and three — no real site, or hours and listings that conflict — without
realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation
that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering driver questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service
pages for extraction](/learn/towing-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and accurate
hours](/learn/towing-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose between them —
you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small tow companies have the advantage?

In availability and reviews. AI names only one or two companies in an emergency and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a local company with accurate 24-hour hours and genuine
reviews can win the direct call over a faceless app. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and
[local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-towing) edge an app can't copy — and a direct call pays full price.
