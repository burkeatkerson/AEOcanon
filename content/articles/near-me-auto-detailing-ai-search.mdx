## Why is 'near me' the highest-value detailing search?

Because it's the moment an owner decides to book. When someone asks "car detailing near me" or "mobile
detailing nearby," they're ready to act — and the [AI answer names only a few
shops](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a link just 8% of the
time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a detailer, owning that moment fills the calendar — and a near-me
booking can become a recurring maintenance client or a high-margin ceramic job.

## What do owners ask AI when choosing a detailer?

Ready-to-book, specific questions — and you should own the ones that fit your services.

## Why does publishing package pricing help so much?

Because owners ask AI "how much is a full detail near me," and a clear price or range in readable text
lets the engine recommend you for those high-intent searches — while competitors who hide pricing get
skipped. Transparency also builds trust before someone hands over their car. It's pure
[Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question, answered where the engine and the owner can read it.
Be specific about packages and pricing, back it with [reviews](/learn/auto-detailing-reviews-ai-recommendations),
and you win the near-me booking.
