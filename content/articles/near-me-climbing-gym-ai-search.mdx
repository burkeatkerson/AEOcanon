## Why is 'near me' the highest-value climbing gym search?

Because it's the moment someone decides to climb, and it can convert into recurring revenue. When a
visitor asks "best climbing gym near me" or "bouldering nearby," they're ready to go — and the [AI
answer names only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people
click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a gym, owning that moment is the difference between a busy floor
and an empty one — and a day-pass visitor who joins keeps paying month after month.

## Why does matching your activity matter so much?

Because visitors search by exactly what they want to do, and a generic gym matches nothing in
particular. The person searching "beginner bouldering near me" or "rec center with a pool nearby" wants
a place that clearly fits — and if your site states your activities and strengths in plain text, the
engine can match you to that specific, high-intent query. A vague "something for everyone" page is
invisible to these searches. Be specific about what you offer and who you're great for, back it with
[reviews that prove it](/learn/rec-center-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the visitors most
likely to come back — the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [Alignment](/pillars/alignment) edge
that fills the floor.
