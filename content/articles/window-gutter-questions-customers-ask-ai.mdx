## How do I find the exact questions?

Listen where homeowners already ask. Note what prospects ask when they call, read your reviews and the
questions on your Google profile, scan home and homeowner forums, and [prompt the assistants
directly](/learn/how-to-find-questions-ai-users-ask) on your services and area to see the follow-ups
they surface. Capture the *natural wording* — "how much to clean gutters on a two-story house" beats
"linear-foot gutter cleaning rates" — because engines match the homeowner's phrasing. Then prioritize by
what you offer.

## Should I answer 'how often should I clean my gutters'?

Yes — frequency content captures intent early and points toward a recurring schedule. When a homeowner
asks AI "how often should I clean my gutters" or "do I need gutter guards," answering helpfully makes you
the cited, trusted source — and a natural call when they decide to book. Frequency and how-to content
earns the click; add-on content wins bigger jobs. Both build the credibility engines
[reward](/learn/how-to-make-content-ai-cant-ignore) — the opposite of a thin photo page. Map every bucket
to a page and you've built the content plan that gets a window & gutter company cited.
