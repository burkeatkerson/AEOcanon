**Write towing service pages AI will cite by giving each service its own page that leads with the answer to
cost, availability, and service-area questions, in plain language a stranded driver and an engine can
lift.** One crawlable page per service wins the full-price call a bloated services page loses.

## Why one page per service?

Because a citation is awarded to the page that best answers one specific question — and a catch-all
services page answers none of them well. When you give emergency towing, roadside assistance, lockouts,
jump-starts, flatbed, heavy-duty, and winch-outs each [their own
page](/learn/should-content-sections-stand-alone), each can go deep on its own cost, availability, and
detail — and each becomes citable for its own query, from 'who can jump my car' to 'flatbed tow near me'. A
single page trying to cover everything is shallow on all of them, so the engine cites a competitor with a
dedicated, focused page and that company wins the call.

## What should each page lead with?

The answer the stranded driver came for, before anything about you. Compare these two openings:

<VersusSplit
  left={{ title: "Weak — buries the answer", items: ["'At Apex Towing we treat every customer like family...'", "Decades of combined experience first", "No price, no hours, no arrival time", "The engine has nothing to quote"] }}
  right={{ title: "Strong — answer-first, citable", items: ["'A local tow at Apex starts at $75 plus $4/mile.'", "'Available 24/7, typically on scene in 30–45 min.'", "'We do lockouts, jumps, flatbed, and winch-outs.'", "The engine lifts it into 'tow truck near me now'"] }}

/>

Then add the detail, the dispatch area, and the proof below. This is [answer-first
writing](/learn/answer-first-sentence) applied to the trade, at its most time-sensitive.

## What makes a service page extractable?

Plain language and a clean structure. Write the way a driver asks — "a local tow typically starts at …"
and "we're available 24 hours and usually arrive in 30–45 minutes" — not in jargon, and use
[question-shaped headings](/learn/question-shaped-headings) the engine can match to a query. Keep each
answer in a self-contained passage so it can be [lifted without the surrounding
page](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage), reinforce it with [LocalBusiness
schema](/learn/towing-schema-markup) and accurate 24-hour hours, and make sure the page is fast and
crawlable. Answer-first, focused, and available — that's the page an engine cites and a stranded driver
calls at full price.
