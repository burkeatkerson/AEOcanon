## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of who and where you are — exactly what an engine
needs to recommend a local garage door company. When the assistant decides whom to name for a
near-me or emergency query, it leans on the profile to verify your identity, service area, hours,
and reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A
complete, accurate profile makes you an easy, safe recommendation; a thin or stale one leaves the
engine unsure and routes the call elsewhere.

Everything an engine checks before vouching for you.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/garage-door-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and trust signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your [actual service pages](/learn/garage-door-service-pages-for-ai) for the
detailed questions a profile can't answer — what a spring repair costs, whether to fix or replace,
the brands you carry. You need both: a complete profile so the engine can confidently place and
trust you, and answer-first pages for the substance that wins the detailed query. Treat them as one
system, kept [consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
