## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you do, and when you're open —
exactly what an engine needs to recommend a studio. When the assistant decides whom to name for a near-me
query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, services, and reputation [before
recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, accurate profile with
real healed-work photos makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine
unsure and routes the client elsewhere.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement signal, but engines also retrieve and
cite your own [readable style and pricing pages](/learn/tattoo-studio-service-pages-for-ai) for
style-level and detailed questions — "fine-line tattoo near me," "studio that does cover-ups." You need
both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you, and readable, described styles and
artists for the substance that wins the style query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
