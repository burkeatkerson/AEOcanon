## Where does the location actually come from?

From several signals at once. Your IP and device geolocation give a rough position,
anything you name in the prompt refines it, and account or profile data tied to your
session can sharpen it further. Engines combine whatever they have to guess where "near
me" is — no single source, just the best available picture.

## How do I make sure I get matched?

Spell out where you operate. Name the cities, neighborhoods, and regions you serve on
real, answer-first pages, and keep those areas consistent with your listings. Engines
match location signals they can read clearly, so a page that plainly states your service
area — reinforced by [consistent local citations](/learn/local-citations-and-ai-search)
— makes you eligible for the near-me questions your customers actually ask.
