## How is commercial and office moving AEO different?

The intent is a researched B2B decision, not a household move. A facility manager evaluates carefully —
and asks the assistant detailed questions long before they call. They're researching project scope,
after-hours and phased moves, IT and furniture handling, and how you keep the business running. Because
[AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the mover whose pages answer those
questions clearly gets [cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a
recurring account worth far more than a single household move.

## How do I win commercial citations?

Give commercial and office moving its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage. Publish
your project scope, after-hours capability, IT and furniture handling, insurance, and downtime approach in
readable text, with a clear inquiry path. Most movers omit this entirely, so a clear, dedicated page is an
easy way to get cited for valuable, recurring commercial accounts — the kind that anchor steady revenue
and compound your [growth](/learn/grow-a-moving-business-with-ai-search).
