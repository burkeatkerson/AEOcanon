## Why does seasonality matter for maid services?

Because cleaning demand spikes in predictable waves, and the cited service catches them. Early spring
brings "spring cleaning service near me"; late fall and the holidays bring hosting prep and pre- and
post-party deep cleans; summer brings move-in and move-out cleaning. The service whose answer is already
published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment each surge
hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors scramble. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a cleaning calendar, and each surge is full of high-intent first
bookings.

## How do I prepare for each season?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'spring cleaning' or 'move-out cleaning' page
[accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the dates and pricing each year,
which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your signals. Keep one
strong page per season, sharpen it before each surge, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/maid-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling when the
rush hits.
