## Why are your programs the most important pages?

Because what you teach is what parents and students search for — and a citation goes to the page the
engine can read. When someone asks "kids karate near me" or "BJJ gym with beginner classes nearby," the
engine matches the query against programs it can [actually
parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site). If your schedule and tuition live only inside a booking
widget, the engine doesn't know what you teach, so you're invisible for those program-level queries — the
most valuable, highest-intent searches there are. Readable programs turn your whole dojo into citable
answers.

## What makes a program page citable?

Readable text, organized the way parents and students think.

## Why describe programs, not just list them?

Because description is the language of fit. Parents ask AI for "a confidence-building program for a shy
kid" or "a beginner-friendly dojo for an out-of-shape adult," and a bare program list gives the engine
little to match. A sentence describing each program — who it's for, what to expect, the benefits — gives
the engine the [extractable detail](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage) that ties you to those
specific, high-intent searches, and mirrors the
[reviews](/learn/martial-arts-school-reviews-ai-recommendations) families write about your instructors. A
readable, described set of programs is the foundation every other dojo AEO move builds on.
