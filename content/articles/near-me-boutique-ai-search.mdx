## Why is near-me the highest-value boutique search?

Because it's the moment of intent, and it converts fast. When someone searches "plus-size boutique near
me" or "vintage clothing shop nearby," they're ready to shop — and the [AI answer names only a few
shops](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a link just 8% of the
time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the visit. For a boutique, owning that moment — especially your specific niche — is
the difference between a busy shop floor and a quiet one.

## What do shoppers ask AI for a nearby boutique?

Specific, style-driven questions — and you should own the ones that match your shop.

Answering these accurately is the [Alignment pillar](/pillars/alignment) at its most local: be the shop
that clearly fits the style, size, and budget and you earn both the citation and the visit. Map each to
your [product and style pages](/learn/boutique-product-pages-for-ai).

## Why are niche and price range the make-or-break signals?

Because near-me boutique searches are rarely generic — they're "plus-size boutique near me" or
"affordable vintage shop nearby," and the engine answers straight from your stated niche and price. A
clear, readable niche and price level means you get recommended for that exact search; a missing or
vague one means you're dropped for a shop that made it obvious. State exactly what you specialize in and
your price range in [plain text](/learn/local-aeo-for-boutiques), keep it consistent with your
[listings](/learn/nap-consistency), and you win the near-me search that's worth the most — the shopper
ready to walk in today.
