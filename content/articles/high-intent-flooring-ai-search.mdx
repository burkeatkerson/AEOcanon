## Why is high-intent the most valuable flooring search?

Because it's the moment money is about to move, and the assistant frames the whole decision. A
homeowner ready to install researches cost per square foot, timeline, and whether old flooring comes
up before they contact anyone — and the [AI answer names only a few
sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a link just 8% of
the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist before you ever hear from them. The installer cited at this stage
is the one the homeowner trusts enough to call first.

## Why does answering cost honestly matter so much?

Because cost is the first thing a homeowner researches and the question most flooring sites dodge. A
clear, honest cost-per-square-foot range by material — with the factors that move it (subfloor prep,
removal, stairs, material grade) — earns trust and citations precisely when intent is highest. Dodging
it ("every floor is different, call us") sends the homeowner to a competitor or a lead-gen platform
that actually answered. Being the installer who explains the money honestly is the
[Credibility](/pillars/credibility) and [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge that gets you cited
and called — map every one of these questions to a page in your
[questions library](/learn/flooring-questions-customers-ask-ai).
