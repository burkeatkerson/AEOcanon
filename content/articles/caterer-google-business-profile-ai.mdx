## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you serve, what you cater, and how clients rate
you — exactly what an engine needs to recommend a caterer. When the assistant decides whom to name for a
near-me or event-type query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, service area, cuisines, and
reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete,
accurate profile makes you an easy recommendation; a thin profile or an unclear service area leaves the
engine unsure — or sends you an inquiry outside the area you actually cover.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and service-area signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [readable packages](/learn/caterer-menu-pages-for-ai) and pages for pricing,
inclusions, and detailed questions — "wedding catering per person," "does the price include staff." You
need both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you and confirm you cover the area, and
readable packages and pages for the substance that wins the high-intent query. Treat them as one system,
kept [consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
