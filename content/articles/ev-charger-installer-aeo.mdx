## Why is EV charging the growth opportunity in electrical?

Because it's new demand, it's recurring, and most of it routes through an AI question
first. EV adoption keeps climbing, and nearly every new owner needs a Level 2 home charger
installed — frequently alongside a [panel or service upgrade](/learn/electrician-service-pages-for-ai)
that raises the job value well above the charger alone. It's high-margin work, the field is
still under-served, and the buyers are researching online before they call. That makes it a
textbook AEO opportunity: be the electrician the assistant [names in the
answer](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) and you capture a steady stream of these
jobs before competitors even know they were searched.
