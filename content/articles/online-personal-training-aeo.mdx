## How is online-training AEO different?

The reach is national, not just near-me. A client looking for virtual coaching isn't bound to your city
— they're asking the assistant for an online trainer who fits their goal, and they research carefully
before committing to a remote program. They want to know how the programming works, what's included, and
whether they'll get results without a local gym. Because [AEO works for this researched
decision](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the trainer whose pages answer those questions
clearly gets [cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a recurring
client from anywhere. And online clients are a scalable stream: you're not limited by hours on a gym
floor.

## How do I win online-training citations?

Give your online or virtual coaching its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage.
Publish how the programming works, what's included, the check-in cadence, real client results, and
pricing in readable text, reinforced by your
[reviews](/learn/personal-trainer-reviews-ai-recommendations) and a clear sign-up path. Most trainers
either omit this entirely or hide it, so a clear, dedicated page is an easy way to get cited for a
scalable, national stream of recurring clients — revenue that compounds your
[growth](/learn/grow-a-personal-training-business-with-ai-search) without adding gym-floor hours.
