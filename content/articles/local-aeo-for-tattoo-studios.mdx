## What makes a tattoo studio 'local' to an engine?

Confidence about where you are and what you do. An answer engine recommends a studio for "near me" only
when it can [place you with certainty](/learn/how-ai-chooses-local-business-to-recommend) — right area,
right kind of work. That certainty comes from signals that all agree: matching name-address-phone,
accurate hours, a complete profile, readable styles and pricing, and reviews. When the signals agree,
you're an easy recommendation; when they conflict or your portfolio is hidden in an image gallery, the
engine favors a clearer competitor.

## Why does NAP consistency matter so much?

Because a mismatch is a doubt, and engines route around doubt. If your phone number differs between your
site and your Google profile, or an old address still shows in a listing, the engine can't be sure which
studio you are or where you operate — so it favors a competitor it can [place with
confidence](/learn/nap-consistency). For studios that have moved, rebranded, or had artists come and go,
this is a common and quiet leak. Clean it up and you remove the single most fixable reason an engine
skips you on a near-me query.
