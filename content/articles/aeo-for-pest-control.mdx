## Why does AEO matter so much for pest control?

Because pest problems are urgent, trust-sensitive, and decided on the first credible answer.
When someone finds bed bugs or a wasp nest, they ask an assistant and act immediately — and
the [AI answer names only two or three sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not
a page of links. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so when someone wants a problem gone the answer *is* the front door. Being the cited company
is the new equivalent of being the first call — and often the start of a recurring plan.

## How do pest control companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a customer — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most pest control companies have a slow site and a reputation that's inconsistent across
listings — so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing
those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that
[ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast,
locally-trusted site that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering questions
[directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service pages for
extraction](/learn/pest-control-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/pest-control-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on. You
don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local pest control companies have the advantage?

In specificity and trust. AI names only a few sources and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a focused local company that answers "how much
to treat bed bugs in [city]" better than a national directory can take the recommendation.
The directory is generic and faceless; you're specific, local, licensed, and real. That's
the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-pest-control)
edge a big aggregator can't easily copy — and a verifiable license plus honest safety
information is trust a directory listing can't show.
