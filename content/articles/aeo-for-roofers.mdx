## Why does AEO matter so much for roofers?

Because roofing is high-ticket, trust-sensitive, and now decided on the first credible
answer. A new roof is a major decision, so homeowners research it — and that research
increasingly starts by asking an assistant, which [names only two or three
sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) rather than a page of links. Pew
Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared,
versus 15% without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/).
For a trade where one job is worth thousands, being the cited roofer is the difference
between a booked estimate and a competitor's.

## How do roofers get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most roofers have a beautiful but slow site and a reputation that's inconsistent across
listings — so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened.
Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that
[ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## How does this help me stop buying leads?

By replacing rented demand with demand you own. A purchased lead is [sold to several
competitors at once](/learn/business-case-for-aeo), so you pay to bid on a job against
the very platform that sold it to you. An AI citation does the opposite — it sends the
homeowner straight to you, for free, and keeps sending them. Build enough
[answer-first content](/learn/roofing-service-pages-that-get-cited) and [local
trust](/learn/local-aeo-for-roofers), and your pipeline shifts from rented to owned —
the whole argument of [growing a roofing business with AI
search](/learn/grow-a-roofing-business-with-ai-search).

## Where do local roofers have the advantage?

In specificity and proof. AI names only a few sources and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a focused local roofer who answers "how
much does a roof replacement cost in [city]" and shows real local jobs, reviews, and
certifications beats a generic national aggregator. The aggregator is broad and
faceless; you're specific, local, certified, and real — the [Originality](/pillars/originality)
and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-roofers) edge a platform can't copy.
