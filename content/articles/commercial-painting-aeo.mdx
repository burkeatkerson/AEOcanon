## How is commercial painting AEO different?

The intent is a researched B2B decision, not a single home. A property or facility manager evaluates
carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before they call. They're researching
experience, scheduling, coatings, and whether you can work around their operations. Because [AEO works
for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the painter whose pages answer those questions
clearly gets [cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a high-value
job that often leads to repeat property work.

## How do I win commercial citations?

Give commercial painting its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage. Publish your
experience, scheduling approach, coatings expertise, insurance, and references in readable text, with a
clear inquiry path. Most painters either omit this or hide it, so a clear, dedicated page is an easy way
to get cited for valuable commercial jobs — the kind that lead to repeat property contracts and compound
your [growth](/learn/grow-a-painting-business-with-ai-search).
