## How is commercial GC AEO different?

The intent is research and risk, not a homeowner remodel. A facility manager, developer, or business
owner evaluates carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before contacting anyone.
They're researching tenant-improvement experience, bonding capacity, and whether you can deliver
their kind of project on time. Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly
here, the contractor whose pages answer those questions expertly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and lands on the shortlist before a single call.
And commercial projects are large, repeatable relationships, so each one won is worth far more than a
single remodel.

## What questions do commercial buyers ask?

Evaluation-focused, technical, and substantive — each worth a thorough page.

## What content wins commercial citations?

Depth backed by proof. Commercial decisions carry higher stakes and longer research cycles, so your
pages should demonstrate genuine expertise — delivery-method knowledge, bonding and safety record,
certifications, and clearly scoped capabilities — in thorough,
[answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence) pages. The fundamentals are the same as residential: be
readable, answer the real question, be trusted. Commercial just rewards [depth and
credibility](/pillars/credibility) more heavily, because the buyer is betting their project and
budget on getting the choice right. Be the page that proves you're the safe expert, and you win the
shortlist.
