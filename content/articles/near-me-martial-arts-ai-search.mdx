## Why is 'near me' the highest-value martial arts search?

Because it's the moment someone decides to enroll, and it converts into recurring tuition. When a parent
asks "best martial arts school near me" or "kids karate nearby," they're ready to commit — and the [AI
answer names only a few schools](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people
click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a dojo, owning that moment is the difference between a full program
and an empty one — and every student enrolled keeps paying month after month for years.

## Why does matching your discipline matter so much?

Because prospects search by exactly what they want, and a generic dojo matches nothing in particular. The
parent searching "kids karate near me" or the adult searching "BJJ gym nearby" wants a place that clearly
fits — and if your site states your disciplines and who they're for in plain text, the engine can match
you to that specific, high-intent query. A vague "we teach martial arts for everyone" page is invisible
to these searches. Be specific about what you teach and who you're great for, back it with [reviews that
prove it](/learn/martial-arts-school-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the prospects most likely
to stay — the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [Alignment](/pillars/alignment) edge that fills
programs.
