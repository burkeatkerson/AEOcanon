## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you work, what you do, and whether you're available
— exactly what an engine needs to recommend a mobile mechanic with no storefront. When the assistant decides
whom to name for a near-me or come-to-you query, it leans on the profile to verify your coverage area, hours,
services, and reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A
complete, accurate service-area profile makes you an easy recommendation; a vague area or a thin profile
leaves the engine unsure who covers the driver's location.

## How should I set up my profile?

As a service-area business, with everything an engine checks before recommending you.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and coverage signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [service pages](/learn/mobile-mechanic-service-pages-for-ai) for the detailed
questions a profile can't answer — what a mobile diagnostic costs, whether you can do brakes on-site, how
your pricing compares to a shop. You need both: a complete, accurate service-area profile so the engine can
place you, and answer-first pages for the substance that wins the detailed query. Treat them as one system,
kept [consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
