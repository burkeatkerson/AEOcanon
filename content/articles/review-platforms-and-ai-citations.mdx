## Why does AI lean on third-party review sites?

Because they're independent evidence it can't get from your own pages. A large body of
genuine reviews on a recognized platform is harder to fake and easier to corroborate,
so engines weigh it more heavily than self-published testimonials. These platforms are
[mentions with reputation attached](/learn/mentions-vs-backlinks) — aggregated sentiment
an engine can lean on when it decides who to recommend.

## Should I chase all of them?

No — focus beats spread. Pick the two or three platforms that fit your category, then
keep a steady flow of genuine, recent reviews there and respond to show you're active.
Credible depth on the sites engines actually read for your business type does far more
than thin coverage on platforms nobody consults for you. Use the
[review-platform playbook](/authority/review-platform-playbook) to prioritize.
