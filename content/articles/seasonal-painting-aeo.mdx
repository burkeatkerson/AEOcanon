## Why does seasonality matter for painters?

Because painting demand swings with the weather, and the cited painter catches each wave. Spring and
summer bring exterior painting searches as the weather allows; fall and winter bring interior projects
and cabinets. The painter whose answer is already published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment
each surge hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors scramble. It's the
[Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a painting calendar, and exterior season is the
biggest, highest-value window to fill.

## How do I prepare for each season?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'exterior painting' or 'cabinet painting'
page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the dates and pricing each
year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your signals.
Keep one strong page per season, sharpen it before each surge, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/painter-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling when
the rush hits.
