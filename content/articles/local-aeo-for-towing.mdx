## What makes a towing company 'local' to an engine?

Confidence about where you are and whether you can come now. An answer engine recommends a tow company for
"near me" only when it can [place you with certainty](/learn/how-ai-chooses-local-business-to-recommend) —
right area, available now. That certainty comes from signals that all agree: matching name-address-phone,
accurate 24-hour hours, a stated service area, and reviews. When the signals agree, you're an easy
recommendation; when your availability conflicts, the engine sends the driver elsewhere.

## How do I win 'near me' in AI answers?

Build location and availability certainty deliberately.

This is the [near-me ranking](/learn/how-to-rank-in-ai-for-near-me-searches) discipline applied to the
trade — and accurate availability is where most companies quietly lose.

## Why does accurate availability matter so much?

Because almost every towing query is urgent — 'tow truck near me now', '24-hour towing' — and the engine
answers it from your stated hours. Accurate, consistent availability means you get recommended when a
driver is stranded; wrong or inconsistent availability means you're dropped — or you send someone to a
company that can't come. Keep your hours and dispatch area accurate and identical everywhere, and you
remove the most common reason a tow company gets skipped. It's the same [NAP
consistency](/learn/nap-consistency) discipline, with availability attached.
