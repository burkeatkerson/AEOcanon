## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, when you're open, and whether you
deliver — exactly what an engine needs to recommend a florist. When the assistant decides whom to name
for a near-me or same-day query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, delivery, and
reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete,
accurate profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine
unsure — or sends a buyer to a shop that's closed.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/florist-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and same-day signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [readable arrangement and occasion pages](/learn/florist-product-pages-for-ai)
for detailed questions — "sympathy arrangements near me," "wedding florist." You need both: a complete,
accurate profile so the engine can place you and confirm delivery, and readable arrangements for the
substance that wins the occasion query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
