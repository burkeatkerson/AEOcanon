**Adaptability is the eighth and final pillar of the AEO Canon: the engines
change monthly, so your doctrine must change with them.** The first seven pillars
tell you what to build. Adaptability is the discipline that keeps the whole
system working as the ground shifts underneath it.

## Why adaptability is the only permanent advantage

Every specific tactic in AEO is provisional. The engines are early, they update
often, and they don't coordinate. A practice that earns citations today can
quietly stop working — not because you did anything wrong, but because the engine
changed. Adaptability is how you survive that.

That volatility is exactly why the Canon leads with durable principles and
treats tactics as disposable. Schema markup and `llms.txt` were widely promoted
as AEO essentials; controlled testing found neither reliably moves citation. The
businesses that bet everything on them had to start over. The ones with adaptable
systems simply updated a hypothesis.

## How to measure AEO (since there's no Search Console for AI)

You can't manage what you can't measure, and per-query checks are too noisy to
trust. The reliable unit is **share of voice**.
