## How is commercial and fleet auto glass AEO different?

The intent is a researched B2B decision, not a retail booking. A fleet manager, dealership service
director, or body-shop owner evaluates carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before
they call. They're researching program scope, on-site service, turnaround, and how you handle ADAS
calibration across a whole fleet. Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here,
the shop whose pages answer those questions clearly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a recurring account worth far more than a
single retail windshield.
