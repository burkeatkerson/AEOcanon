**Hobby shops get found by AI search when their brands and what they carry are readable as real text,
they list their events, they answer the questions customers ask, and they're backed by accurate
listings and genuine reviews.** The AI names only a few stores, so the shop that clears all three is the
one recommended.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When someone wants a hobby shop, the engine pulls the stores it can
read, judges which best fit the request and look well-reviewed, and [names just a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Each step is a filter: if the crawler can't read your
brands, you're out before ranking; if your hours are wrong, you're dropped from near-me queries; if your
reviews are thin, a competitor gets named. Being found means clearing every filter — and for hobby
shops, the readable brands and listed events are often what's missing.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a customer — apply, in order.

## Why can't customers find my hobby shop?

Almost always one specific broken gate — and for hobby shops it's usually the image-only, social-only
site. If your brands and events live only in [photos and Facebook posts the crawler can't
read](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), the engine doesn't know what you carry or what you run,
so it can't recommend you for "game store with Friday Night Magic near me" or "shop that carries
Warhammer." Other common breaks: inconsistent hours, or thin reviews. The fix is diagnostic — find the
*highest* broken gate and repair it first. For most hobby shops, putting brands and events in real text
is the highest-leverage move.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking and a map listing help, but AI citation also needs your [brands and events in
readable text](/learn/hobby-shop-product-pages-for-ai) and genuine reviews. A store can show on a map
yet be absent from "best game store near me" answers if the engine can't read the site or the reviews
are thin. Clear all three gates and you get [found in both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
