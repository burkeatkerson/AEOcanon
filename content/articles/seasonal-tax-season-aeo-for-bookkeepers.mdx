## Why is tax season the biggest AEO lever for a firm?

Because demand for tax and accounting help spikes hard and predictably, and the cited firm catches the
surge. From January through April, owners search "tax filing deadline," "what do I need to file my
small-business taxes," and "do I need an accountant" in huge volume. The firm whose answer is already
published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the spike hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo)
— while competitors are still scrambling. It's the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a
tax calendar, and the single biggest demand window of the year.

## How do I prepare for the surge?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when search volume climbs.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per topic. A persistent 'small-business tax deadlines' or 'what to bring
to your tax appointment' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh
the dates and figures each year, which beats a throwaway page that starts from zero annually and splits
your signals. Keep one strong page per tax topic, refresh it ahead of the season, and link it to your
[service pages](/learn/bookkeeper-service-pages-for-ai) — one durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the surge hits.
