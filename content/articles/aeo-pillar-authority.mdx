**Authority is the fourth pillar of the AEO Canon: AI trusts what the web
already trusts, and it weighs mentions more heavily than backlinks.** This is the
first pillar of the Reputation layer — and the place where AEO diverges most
sharply from a decade of link-building habits.

## Why authority lives off your own site

You can claim anything on your own pages. Engines know this, so they corroborate
— they look at how the rest of the web describes you. Authority is the web
vouching for you, and it is earned in the places you don't control.

<PullQuote cite="Pillar 4 · Authority">
  You are what the web says you are.
</PullQuote>

<Callout variant="insight" title="The metaphor">
  <p>
    Authority is word of mouth at internet scale. The machine is listening to
    the room — Reddit threads, YouTube videos, Wikipedia, the publications that
    say your name unprompted. Your job is to give the room something true to say
    about you.
  </p>
</Callout>

## The evidence: mentions outweigh links

Ahrefs studied 75,000 brands and measured how different signals correlate with
AI visibility. Backlinks — the cornerstone of traditional SEO — landed fourth.

The pattern is consistent: signals that show the web _talking about you_ outrank
the signal that shows the web _linking to you_. Reddit is among the most-cited
domains across engines, with Wikipedia, YouTube, and LinkedIn close behind.
