## Why are case studies the strongest proof for an agency?

Because an agency's whole pitch is results, and a published case study is the verifiable proof an engine
can read. When an assistant decides whom to recommend, it leans on the [off-site and on-site
evidence](/learn/mentions-vs-backlinks) that you deliver — and a crawlable page naming a real client
situation, the work, and a measurable outcome is exactly that evidence. It's the
[Credibility](/pillars/credibility) and [Authority](/pillars/authority) pillars made concrete, and it
directly answers the [ready-to-hire question](/learn/high-intent-marketing-agency-ai-search) buyers ask:
"what results have you actually driven?"

## What makes a case study work for AEO?

Structure and real numbers, on a page an engine can read.

<Steps
  items={[
    { title: "Lead with the result", body: "Open with the measurable outcome — 'doubled qualified leads in six months', 'cut CAC 40%' — in plain numbers an engine can lift, not a vague 'we elevated their brand'." },
    { title: "Name the situation", body: "The client's industry, size, and starting point, so the case study matches niche 'agency for [industry]' searches and feels real." },
    { title: "Show the work", body: "What you actually did — the channels, the strategy, the timeline — so the page proves competence, not just a happy ending." },
    { title: "Make it crawlable", body: "A dedicated, text-rich page per engagement, not an image-only slider or logo wall a crawler can't read or a buyer can't skim." },
  ]}
/>

A case study buried in a homepage carousel or rendered only as images is invisible to the engine —
[the same access problem](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site) that sinks showcase sites. Put each
one on its own readable page and it gets cited for its industry and its result.

## Where should case studies live?

On their own crawlable pages, woven into the rest of your site. Each case study page should link to the
matching [service page](/learn/marketing-agency-service-pages-for-ai) and
[specialization page](/learn/niche-marketing-agency-aeo), so a SaaS case study reinforces your "agency
for SaaS" claim and your SEO case study backs your SEO service. The reviews that
[name the same results](/learn/marketing-agency-reviews-ai-recommendations) corroborate the case study,
and together they build the [compounding proof](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) that earns citations and
[grows recurring retainers](/learn/grow-a-marketing-agency-with-ai-search). Publish results honestly and
readably, and they do your AEO for you — while proving to the next buyer you can do the same for them.
