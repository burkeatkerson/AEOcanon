## Why does AEO matter so much for studios?

Because choosing a yoga or Pilates studio is a local, high-consideration decision, and the answer is
the new front door. When someone decides to start, they ask an assistant and act on what it names — and
the [AI answer names only two or three places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of
listings. Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared,
versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited studio is the new equivalent of being the studio
everyone recommends — and each member is recurring revenue, not a one-time drop-in.

## How do studios get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a student — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

<Steps
  items={[

    { title: "Answer the real question (Alignment + Extractability)", body: "Plain-text answers to 'how much is a membership', 'do you have beginner classes', 'hot yoga or vinyasa', 'reformer or mat Pilates', 'do you offer prenatal', and 'where are you'." },
    { title: "Be trusted (Authority + Credibility)", body: "Consistent name, address, and phone everywhere; a complete Google Business Profile; and genuine, recent reviews that mention teachers, class styles, and how students felt. This is what decides who gets named." },

Most studios fail gate one without knowing it — the schedule and prices live in a booking widget the
engine can't read. Fixing that, plus accurate listings and reviews, is the highest-leverage work, and
it's the same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed studio with a readable schedule and pricing that ranks is the same one that gets
[cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds putting your class styles, schedule, and answers in
[plain text](/learn/answer-first-sentence), [structured data](/learn/yoga-studio-schema-markup), and
the [review consistency](/learn/yoga-studio-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You
don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do independent studios have the advantage?

In specificity and community. AI names only a few studios and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so an independent with a readable schedule, clear pricing,
and genuine reviews naming teachers and how students felt can win the recommendation over a faceless
chain or aggregator listing. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-yoga-studios) edge a chain can't copy — your community and your
teachers are exactly what a student is looking for.
