## Why are package pages the most important page?

Because what you offer and what it costs is what clients search for — and a citation goes to the page the
engine can read. When someone asks "how much is a wedding photographer" or "who does engagement and family
sessions near me," the engine matches the query against pages it can [actually
parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site). If your packages are an image-only gallery, a PDF, or hidden
behind a contact form, the engine doesn't know what you do, so you're invisible for every high-intent query —
the most valuable searches there are. Readable packages turn your whole offer into citable answers, while your
photos still do the selling.

## What makes a package page citable?

Readable text, organized the way clients think — sitting right alongside your best images.

## Why publish pricing and what's included, not just photos?

Because price and inclusions are the language of the photography decision. Clients ask AI "how much is a
wedding photographer" and "does the package include a second shooter and album," and a beautiful gallery gives
the engine little to match — and leaves the client guessing. Stating your package tiers, starting prices,
turnaround, and exactly what's included gives the engine the [extractable
detail](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage) that ties you to those exact searches, pre-qualifies the
inquiry, and mirrors the [wedding and session reviews](/learn/photographer-reviews-ai-recommendations) clients
write. A readable, priced, detailed package page — shown with your work — is the foundation every other
photography AEO move builds on.
