## Why does AEO matter so much for pool companies?

Because pool problems are urgent, recurring, and decided on the first credible answer. When a pool
turns green before a party or a pump dies, the owner asks an assistant and acts immediately — and the
[AI answer names only two or three companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of
listings. Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared,
versus 15% without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the call. Being the cited company is the new equivalent of being the pool guy a
neighbor recommends — and each account is recurring revenue.

## How do pool companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates an owner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most companies have a thin site and inconsistent listings — so they fail gates one and three without
realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation
that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering owner questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service
pages for extraction](/learn/pool-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/pool-service-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small crews have the advantage?

In specificity and reviews. AI names only a few companies and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a small local crew that answers "how much is weekly pool
service in [city]" and shows real reviews can win the recommendation over a faceless directory. That's
the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-pool-services) edge
an aggregator can't copy — and a recurring account compounds for years.
