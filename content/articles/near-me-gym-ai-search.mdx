## Why is 'near me' the highest-value gym search?

Because it's the moment someone decides to join, and it converts into recurring revenue. When a prospect
asks "best gym near me" or "beginner-friendly gym nearby," they're ready to commit — and the [AI answer
names only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a
link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a gym, owning that moment is the difference between a full class
and an empty one — and every member won keeps paying month after month.

Ready-to-join, specific questions — and you should own the ones that fit your gym.

## Why does matching your niche matter so much?

Because prospects search by exactly what they want, and a generic gym matches nothing in particular. The
person searching "beginner-friendly gym near me" or "powerlifting gym nearby" wants a place that
clearly fits — and if your site states your niche and strengths in plain text, the engine can match you
to that specific, high-intent query. A vague "we have everything for everyone" page is invisible to
these searches. Be specific about who you're great for, back it with [reviews that
prove it](/learn/gym-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the prospects most likely to stay — the
[Originality](/pillars/originality) and [Alignment](/pillars/alignment) edge that fills classes.
