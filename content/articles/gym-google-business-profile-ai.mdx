## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you offer, and when you're open
— exactly what an engine needs to recommend a gym. When the assistant decides whom to name for a
near-me query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, offerings, and reputation
[before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, accurate
profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine unsure and
routes the prospect elsewhere.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement signal, but engines also retrieve and
cite your own [readable class and pricing pages](/learn/gym-service-pages-for-ai) for program-level and
detailed questions — "beginner CrossFit near me," "gym with childcare." You need both: a complete,
accurate profile so the engine can place you, and readable offerings for the substance that wins the
program query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
