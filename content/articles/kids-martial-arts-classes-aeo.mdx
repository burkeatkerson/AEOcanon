## Why is the kids program your highest-value AEO target?

Because a child who starts young stays for years — and the parent decides with AI. Kids and family
programs are where dojos enroll the most students and retain them the longest, all the way through belt
progression. A parent setting out to enroll their child researches carefully and asks the assistant
detailed questions long before they visit. The dojo whose pages answer those questions clearly gets
[cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a family most schools
never even surface for. And a child enrolled at five who trains for years is worth far more than a single
adult drop-in.

## What questions do parents ask?

Reassurance-and-fit, specific, and emotional — each worth a clear answer on a dedicated page.

## How do I win kids and family citations?

Give kids classes, after-school, and family enrollment their own answer-first pages — not a buried line
on your schedule. Publish the ages you serve, the discipline and why it suits children, the confidence
and focus benefits, your safety approach, and how a trial class works in readable text, reinforced by
your [reviews that mention kids' progress](/learn/martial-arts-school-reviews-ai-recommendations) and a
clear trial-booking path. Most dojos either bury this in a widget or omit it, so a clear, dedicated page
is an easy way to get cited for the highest-value, longest-retained enrollments — families that compound
your [growth](/learn/grow-a-martial-arts-school-with-ai-search).
