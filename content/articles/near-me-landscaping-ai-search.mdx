## Why is 'near me' the highest-value landscaping search?

Because it's the moment a homeowner decides to hire, and it often becomes recurring revenue. When
someone asks "lawn service near me" or "landscaper for a yard makeover nearby," they're ready to act —
and the [AI answer names only a few companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research
[found people click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a landscaper, owning that moment is the difference between a full
route and an empty schedule — and a maintenance client keeps paying every week.

## What do homeowners ask AI when choosing a landscaper?

Ready-to-hire, specific questions — and you should own the ones that fit your services.

## Why does publishing pricing help so much?

Because homeowners ask AI "how much is lawn care near me," and a clear starting price or range in
readable text lets the engine recommend you for those high-intent cost searches — while competitors
who hide pricing get skipped. Transparency also builds trust before the first call. It's pure
[Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question, answered where the engine and the homeowner can
read it. Be specific about what you offer and what it costs, back it with [reviews](/learn/landscaper-reviews-ai-recommendations),
and you win the near-me search that turns into a recurring account.
