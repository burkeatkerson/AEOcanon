I do"), cost ("how much to replace a water heater"), local ("plumber near me open
now"), and how-to ("why is my water pressure low").** Mapping each question to the page
that should own it is the core of a plumbing AEO content plan.

## What are the four question buckets?

Plumbing demand sorts cleanly into four kinds of question, each needing a different
page. **Emergency** — "burst pipe what do I do," "water heater leaking" — is urgent and
high-intent. **Cost** — "how much to replace a water heater," "cost to unclog a main
drain" — is the research that precedes a booking. **Local** — "plumber near me open
now," "do you serve [town]" — is placement-driven. And **how-to** — "why is my water
pressure low," "how to stop a running toilet" — is informational but credibility-
building. Each maps to a page, which is the [Alignment pillar](/pillars/alignment)
applied to the trade.

## How do I find my customers' actual questions?

Go where they already ask. Mine your phone and service-call logs for the questions
people genuinely ask, check Reddit, community forums, and People Also Ask for the
phrasing, and [prompt the assistants directly](/learn/how-to-find-questions-ai-users-ask)
on your services to harvest the follow-up questions they surface. Capture the natural
wording — "why is my water brown" beats "discolored water causes" — and prioritize by
intent and value.

## Should I answer how-to questions that don't book a job?

Yes — they're how you earn the booking later. Answering "why is my water pressure low"
helpfully makes you the trusted source a customer returns to when the DIY fix fails and
they need a pro. That [helpful, genuinely useful content](/learn/how-to-make-content-ai-cant-ignore)
is exactly what engines reward with citations, and it builds the credibility that
converts when the question turns urgent. Skipping it cedes the relationship to whoever
did answer.

## How do I turn the list into content?

Map and group. Assign each question to the one page that should own it, group closely
related questions onto [comprehensive pages](/learn/how-many-questions-per-page), and
answer each in a [self-contained, answer-first passage](/learn/answer-first-sentence).
High-value emergency and cost questions earn [dedicated service
pages](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai); minor variations live as passages within
them. That mapped, prioritized backlog is the content plan — and producing it every
month is what the [done-for-you program](/pricing) handles for you.
