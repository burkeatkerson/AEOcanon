**AEO for commercial cleaning means becoming the company AI assistants name when a facility or office
manager needs a cleaning service — by being crawlable, answering the real pricing-and-scope questions,
and earning trust through insurance, certifications, and reviews.** The reward is a recurring
janitorial contract instead of a lead resold to three competitors.

    fetch), <strong>answer the real question</strong> (cost per square foot, frequency, "insured and
    bonded", "do you clean after hours"), and <strong>be trusted</strong> (stated certifications,
    consistent listings, genuine reviews). The AI names a <strong>few</strong> companies — do all three
    and you're one of them, not a resold lead.
  </p>

## Why does AEO matter so much for commercial cleaning?

Because letting a crew into your building after hours is a trust decision, made on the first credible
answer. When a manager decides they need a company, they ask an assistant and act on what it names —
and the [AI answer names only two or three companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a
page of listings. Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited company is the new equivalent of being the name a
fellow manager refers — and each account is recurring monthly revenue.

## How do commercial cleaning companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a manager — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most companies have a thin brochure site and inconsistent listings — so they fail gates one and three
without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same
foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering manager questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service pages for extraction](/learn/commercial-cleaning-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the
[reviews and certifications](/learn/commercial-cleaning-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean
on. You don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small companies have the advantage?

In specificity and trust. AI names only a few companies and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a small local company that answers "how much does medical
office cleaning cost" or "do you do post-construction cleanup" better than a directory can win the
recommendation. The directory is generic; you're specific, certified, and real. That's the
[Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-commercial-cleaning)
edge a faceless aggregator can't copy — and a [high-value niche](/learn/industry-niche-commercial-cleaning-aeo)
sharpens it further.
