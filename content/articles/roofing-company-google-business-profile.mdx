## Why does the profile matter so much for roofers?

Because roofing is a high-trust, high-ticket decision, and the profile is where an engine
confirms you're real. A complete [Google Business
Profile](https://support.google.com/business/answer/7091) anchors who and where you are —
category, service area, services, certifications — and that data shapes how Google's AI
surfaces and the wider local web understand you. For a [recognition-first
trade](/learn/local-aeo-for-roofers), a thin profile is a quiet, recurring loss.

## What should I complete on it?

All of it, accurately and matched to your site.

## Is the profile enough on its own?

No — it's the foundation, not the whole roof. The profile confirms who and where you
are, but citations also depend on [genuine reviews](/learn/roofer-reviews-and-reputation),
verifiable certifications, [consistent listings](/learn/local-aeo-for-roofers), and
[pages that answer roofing questions](/learn/roofing-service-pages-that-get-cited). Get
the profile right first, then build the trust and content layers on top — together they
make you the roofer AI [chooses to recommend](/learn/get-roofing-company-recommended-by-ai).
