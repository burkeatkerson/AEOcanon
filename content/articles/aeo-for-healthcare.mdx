**Healthcare is a high-trust (YMYL) field where AI weighs medical expertise and
reputation heavily, and engines answer from health directories, reviews, and
authoritative medical sources — so AEO for healthcare means demonstrating real
expertise and earning presence on the platforms patients and engines trust, within
HIPAA and advertising rules.** Credibility isn't a tactic here; it's the price of
entry.

## What query patterns do patients use?

Patients ask AI two kinds of questions: **informational** ("symptoms of [condition]",
"is [treatment] safe", "what to expect from [procedure]") and **provider-finding**
("best [specialist] near me", "[clinic] reviews", "does [provider] take [insurance]").
The informational queries are dominated by authoritative medical sources, so your
opportunity there is genuinely expert, well-sourced content; the provider-finding
queries are where a credentialed, well-reviewed local presence wins. Map your real
patient questions and answer them clearly — [alignment](/pillars/alignment) under
unusually high trust requirements.

## What should you build first?

Build trust signals before volume, with compliance reviewing as you go:

  items={[

In a YMYL field, two excellent, credentialed, compliant pages outweigh twenty
generic ones — and every step should pass compliance review before it publishes.
