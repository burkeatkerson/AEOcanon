**Plumbers get found by AI search when their site is crawlable, answers the real
questions customers ask, and is backed by consistent local trust signals.** The AI
retrieves and ranks the few sources that clear all three, so the plumber who does is
the one named in the answer.

## What does "found by AI" actually mean for a plumber?

It means being named in the answer, not buried on a list. When a customer asks an
assistant for a plumber, the engine [retrieves a pool of sources and names a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — so "found" is binary in a way ranking
never was. You're either one of the cited names or you're absent. The job is to be one
of the few the engine trusts enough to say out loud.

## Why can't customers find my plumbing business in AI answers?

Almost always one specific broken gate. The common causes, in order: AI crawlers can't
read your site because it's slow or [rendered so bots see an empty
page](/learn/javascript-breaks-ai-citation); your pages don't directly answer the
question asked; or your reviews and listings are thin or inconsistent. Diagnose
top-down and [fix the highest break first](/learn/why-isnt-my-site-cited-by-ai) — a
great reputation can't save a site the engine can't read.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking proves Google can read and rank you, but AI citation also needs
an [answer-first, extractable page](/learn/answer-first-sentence) and trusted local
signals. A plumber can rank well yet be absent from AI answers if the answer is buried
mid-page or off-site trust is thin — which is why AEO and SEO
[run as one program](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) rather than one replacing the
other.

## What's the fastest way to get found?

Clear the three most common gates. [Confirm AI crawlers can read your
site](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), rewrite your top
[service pages to answer the question first](/learn/plumbing-service-pages-for-ai), and
make your [name, address, and phone identical everywhere](/learn/local-aeo-for-plumbers).
Those moves clear access, relevance, and the baseline of local trust — the rest of the
[plumbing library](/learn/aeo-for-plumbers) builds from there.
