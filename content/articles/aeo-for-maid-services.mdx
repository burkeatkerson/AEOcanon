## Why does AEO matter so much for maid services?

Because letting someone into your home is a trust decision made on the first credible answer. When a
client decides to hire a maid service, they ask an assistant and act on what it names — and the [AI answer
names only two or three services](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings. Pew
Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the booking. Being the cited service is the new equivalent of being the maid service
a neighbor recommends — and each client is recurring revenue.

## How do maid services get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a client — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most companies have a thin or widget-only site and inconsistent listings — so they fail gates one and
three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the
same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering client questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service and pricing pages for extraction](/learn/maid-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews
and listings](/learn/maid-service-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small maid services have the advantage?

In specificity and trust. AI names only a few services and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a small local maid service that answers "how much is a
recurring cleaning in [city]" and shows real, insured, well-reviewed credentials can win the
recommendation over a faceless app. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-maid-services) edge an aggregator can't copy — and a recurring client
compounds for years.
