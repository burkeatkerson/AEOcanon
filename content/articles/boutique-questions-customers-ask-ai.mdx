**Shoppers ask AI boutique questions in four buckets — fit ('do you carry plus sizes'), style ('vintage
denim shop near me'), price ('boutique dresses under $100'), and policy ('do you ship', 'return
policy').** Mapping each to readable content that answers it is the core of a boutique AEO content plan.

## How do I find the exact questions?

Listen where shoppers already ask. Note what customers ask in the shop and over DMs, read your reviews
and the questions on your Google profile, scan style and fashion forums, and [prompt the assistants
directly](/learn/how-to-find-questions-ai-users-ask) on your niche and area to see the follow-ups they
surface. Capture the *natural wording* — "where can I find cute plus-size dresses near me" beats "curve
apparel retail" — because engines match the shopper's phrasing. Then prioritize by what you actually
carry.

## Should I answer 'what should I wear for X' style questions?

Yes — they capture shoppers at the very start, before they've picked a store. When someone asks AI "what
should I wear to a fall wedding" or "what styles flatter a pear shape," answering helpfully makes you
the cited, trusted source — and lets you guide them straight to pieces you carry. Style-guidance content
is pure [Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question, answered where the engine and the shopper
can read it. Map every bucket to readable content and you've built the plan that gets a boutique cited.
