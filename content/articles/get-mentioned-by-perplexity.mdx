of the answer engines.** Strong classic SEO ports to Perplexity better than to any
other engine, which makes it the most predictable place to earn AI visibility.

## How does Perplexity pick what to cite?

Perplexity picks citations by retrieving live web passages against its own index,
reranking them for the query, and quoting the sources it actually uses — then
displaying those citations prominently, since attribution is core to its product.
It runs the universal [retrieve-rerank-cite
pipeline](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), but two things stand out: it
relies on **its own crawler and index** rather than borrowing another engine's, and
it weights **diversity and recency** heavily. There's little of the "answer from
memory" behavior that [ChatGPT](/learn/how-to-get-cited-by-chatgpt) shows — if you
aren't retrievable, you aren't cited.

## Why is Perplexity the most SEO-aligned engine?

Perplexity is the most SEO-aligned engine because its retrieval correlates closely
with classic search relevance — the pages that rank well organically tend to be the
pages it retrieves and cites. That makes traditional SEO unusually transferable
here: crawlability, topical relevance, internal linking, and authority all do
double duty. If you already invest in SEO, Perplexity is where that work converts to
AI citations most directly — more so than for the Bing-grounded engines or Google's
own AI surfaces, which overlap with each other and with Perplexity only ~11%
([Profound](/learn/per-engine-vs-blended-measurement)).

## Does Perplexity have its own crawler?

Yes — **PerplexityBot** indexes the web for Perplexity, and **Perplexity-User**
fetches pages in real time when a user's question requires it. Allow both in
robots.txt so you're eligible to be retrieved and cited; if PerplexityBot can't read
your rendered HTML, you're not in the candidate set at all. This is the
[access](/pillars/access) pillar, and the specifics are in [which AI crawlers
should you allow?](/learn/which-ai-crawlers-to-allow) and the [robots.txt
guide](/learn/allow-ai-crawlers-robots-txt).

## What sources does Perplexity favor?

Perplexity favors a mix of high-trust community sites, reference works, and primary
sources — with a pronounced **Reddit** lean. Reddit is the single most-cited domain
across AI engines, and Perplexity draws on it heavily alongside Wikipedia, YouTube,
authoritative publications, and original data. So your genuine
[authority](/pillars/authority) — being talked about and corroborated across trusted
sites — strongly affects whether Perplexity reaches for you. Go deeper in [do I need
to be on Reddit to get cited by AI?](/learn/reddit-ai-citations)

## How much does freshness matter?

Freshness matters a lot, especially for time-sensitive queries, because Perplexity
emphasizes current information and retrieves in real time.
[Seer found 65% of AI crawler visits target content under a year
old](https://www.seerinteractive.com/insights/study-ai-brand-visibility-and-content-recency),
and recency is one of Perplexity's clearest biases. Substantively updating your key
pages and showing a visible last-updated date is a direct
[freshness](/pillars/freshness) lever for staying citable here.

## Where on the page should the answer go?

Lead with the answer, in a self-contained passage of roughly 120–180 words under a
question-shaped heading — the same [extractability](/pillars/extractability)
discipline that wins every engine. Perplexity's citation-forward UI means a clean,
liftable, well-sourced passage is immediately visible to users as the cited source,
so the payoff for tight answer-first writing is especially direct.

## Where this fits in the Canon

Perplexity rewards the full Canon, but leans hardest on [access](/pillars/access)
(its own crawler must reach you), [freshness](/pillars/freshness) (recency bias),
and [authority](/pillars/authority) (Reddit and trusted sources) — with classic SEO
doing more work here than anywhere else. Measure it with [per-engine share of
voice](/learn/per-engine-vs-blended-measurement).
