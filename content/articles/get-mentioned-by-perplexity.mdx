current, and earn presence on the high-trust sources it favors — Perplexity

citation-forward engine, which makes it a great place to earn visibility with
genuinely strong content.

## How does Perplexity pick what to cite?

Perplexity picks citations by retrieving live web passages, reranking them for the
query, and quoting the sources it actually uses — then displaying those citations
prominently, since attribution is core to its product. Like every answer engine,
it runs a [retrieve-rerank-cite pipeline](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations);
what stands out is how visibly it surfaces sources and how much it weights
diversity and recency.

## What sources does Perplexity favor?

Perplexity favors a mix of high-trust community sites, reference works, and
primary sources. Reddit, Wikipedia, and YouTube rank among the most-cited domains
across AI engines, and Perplexity draws on them heavily alongside authoritative
publications and original data. The practical implication: your own genuine
[authority](/pillars/authority) — being talked about and corroborated
across trusted sites — strongly affects whether Perplexity reaches for you.

## How much does freshness matter?

Freshness matters a lot, especially for anything time-sensitive. Answer engines
weight recent content, and
[Seer found 65% of AI crawler visits target content under a year old](https://www.seerinteractive.com/insights/study-ai-brand-visibility-and-content-recency).
Perplexity's emphasis on current information makes
[freshness](/pillars/freshness) — substantively updating pages and
showing a visible date — a direct lever for staying citable there.

## Do I need a Perplexity-specific strategy?

No — the same fundamentals win across engines. Perplexity uses the same pipeline
as ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews, so answer-first, evidenced, crawlable,
authoritative content competes everywhere. The only Perplexity-specific emphases
are recency and source diversity, both of which you address through freshness and
genuine off-site presence. Start from [what is AEO](/learn/what-is-aeo).

## What should I do first?

Update and tighten your most important page: make the answer lead, add a current
stat or source, show a visible last-updated date, and confirm it's crawlable. Then
keep building genuine mentions on the trusted sources Perplexity reads. Current,
credible, well-sourced answers are exactly what a citation-forward engine wants to
show.
