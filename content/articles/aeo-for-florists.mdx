your arrangements and delivery info readable, answering the real same-day-and-occasion questions, and

service.

## Why does AEO matter so much for florists?

Because flower-buying is local, occasion-driven, and often urgent — decided on the first good answer.
When someone needs an arrangement, they ask an assistant and order from what it names — and the [AI
answer names only two or three shops](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings.
Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the order. Being the cited florist is the new equivalent of being the shop everyone
recommends — and a direct order keeps the full margin.

## How do florists get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a buyer — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

    { title: "Answer the real question (Alignment + Extractability)", body: "Plain-text answers to 'do you deliver same-day', 'do you do sympathy and funeral flowers', 'do you deliver to [town]', and 'how much is a bouquet'." },

Most florists fail gate one without knowing it — the arrangements and delivery info live only in
images. Fixing that, plus accurate listings and reviews, is the highest-leverage work, and it's the
same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed shop with readable arrangements that ranks is the same one that gets
[cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds putting your occasions and delivery info in [plain
text](/learn/answer-first-sentence), [structured data](/learn/florist-schema-markup), and the [review
consistency](/learn/florist-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do independent shops have the advantage?

In specificity and authenticity. AI names only a few shops and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so an independent with readable arrangements, clear same-day
delivery, and genuine reviews can win the recommendation over a faceless wire service that isn't even
local. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-florists) edge an order-gatherer can't copy — and every direct order
keeps the margin.

## What should a florist do first?

Start at the binding constraint. [Make your arrangements and delivery info
readable](/learn/florist-product-pages-for-ai) as real text, then fix your [hours and delivery
area](/learn/local-aeo-for-florists) so same-day is accurate, then build [genuine
reviews](/learn/florist-reviews-ai-recommendations). Work down the rest of this library from there —
[same-day delivery searches](/learn/same-day-flower-delivery-ai-search),
[Google Business Profile](/learn/florist-google-business-profile-ai),
[schema](/learn/florist-schema-markup), and the
[questions buyers actually ask](/learn/florist-questions-customers-ask-ai).
