## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of who and where you are — exactly what an engine
needs to recommend a local locksmith, and exactly what the fake-listing operators can't legitimately
produce. When the assistant decides whom to name for a near-me or lockout query, it leans on the
profile to verify your identity, service area, hours, and reputation [before recommending
you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, genuine, verified profile makes
you an easy, safe recommendation; a thin or stale one leaves the engine unsure.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/locksmith-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and trust signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your [actual service pages](/learn/locksmith-service-pages-for-ai) for the detailed
questions a profile can't answer — what a lockout costs, whether you can make a specific car key, how
rekeying works. You need both: a complete profile so the engine can confidently place and trust you,
and answer-first pages for the substance that wins the detailed query. Treat them as one system, kept
[consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
