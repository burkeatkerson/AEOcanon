## Why does seasonality matter for plumbing AEO?

Because plumbing demand is weather- and calendar-driven, and the citation goes to
whoever's already current when the spike hits. Frozen pipes in a cold snap, sump pumps
in spring storms, water heaters before the holidays — each is a predictable surge of
[urgent questions](/learn/emergency-plumber-ai-search). The plumber who already has the
cited answer when the cold snap arrives wins the calls; the one who starts writing after
it's freezing is too late, because [engines need time to crawl and
trust](/learn/how-fast-does-ai-pick-up-new-content) a page.

## When should I publish seasonal content?

Ahead of the season, every time. Publish or refresh your frozen-pipe content in fall,
your storm and sump-pump content before spring, your water-heater readiness before the
holidays. The [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) here is about *leading* the demand
curve — giving engines weeks to crawl, index, and trust the page so it's ready to be
cited the moment customers start asking. Refresh on a schedule tied to your climate,
not the calendar in the abstract.

## New page each year, or update one?

Update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent "how to prevent frozen pipes
in [area]" URL [accumulates authority](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) while
you refresh it each winter with current details — far better than a new throwaway page
annually that starts from zero and [splits your
signals](/learn/does-duplicate-content-hurt-aeo). Keep the URL, update the content, and
refresh the date honestly. The page gets stronger every year instead of resetting.

## Which seasonal questions should I own?

The ones that track the weather and calendar. Map each recurring seasonal question —
preventing and handling frozen and burst pipes, sump-pump failures in storms,
winterizing plumbing, water-heater readiness before guests arrive — to a durable page
you own and refresh, using the [question-mapping
approach](/learn/plumbing-questions-customers-ask-ai). Running these seasonal campaigns
on time, every year, is exactly the kind of ongoing work the
[done-for-you program](/pricing) handles so you don't miss a season.
