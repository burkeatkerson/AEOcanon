## How do I prepare for each spike?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand turns. Publish or refresh your winter
crack-spread page in late fall, your rock-chip page in late spring — ahead of when drivers search. Pages
need to be [crawled and trusted](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) before the spike; reacting
after the first cold snap is too late to be cited in the moment. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into an auto glass calendar.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'crack spreading in cold weather' or
'rock chip repair' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the
details each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your
signals. Keep one strong page per topic, sharpen it before each spike, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/auto-glass-shop-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the surge hits.
