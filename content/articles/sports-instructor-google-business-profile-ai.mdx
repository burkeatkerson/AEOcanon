## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you teach, what you offer, and when you're
available — exactly what an engine needs to recommend a coach. When the assistant decides whom to name
for a near-me query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, lessons, and reputation
[before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, accurate
profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine unsure and
routes the student elsewhere.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/sports-instructor-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement signal, but engines also retrieve and
cite your own [readable lesson and pricing pages](/learn/sports-instructor-service-pages-for-ai) for
program-level and detailed questions — "beginner golf lessons near me," "junior tennis program." You
need both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you, and readable lessons for the
substance that wins the program query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
