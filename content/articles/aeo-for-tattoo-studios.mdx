## Why does AEO matter so much for tattoo studios?

Because choosing a tattoo artist is a local, high-trust decision, and the answer is the new front door.
When someone decides to get inked, they ask an assistant and act on what it names — and the [AI answer
names only two or three studios](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings. Pew
Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited studio is the new equivalent of being the shop
everyone recommends — and a client who loves their piece is repeat work and referrals, not a one-time
sale.

## How do tattoo studios get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a client — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most studios fail gate one without knowing it — the portfolio and prices live in an image gallery the
engine can't read. Fixing that, plus accurate listings and reviews, is the highest-leverage work, and
it's the same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed studio with readable styles and pricing that ranks is the same one that gets
[cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds putting your portfolio descriptions and answers in [plain
text](/learn/answer-first-sentence), [structured data](/learn/tattoo-studio-schema-markup), and the
[review consistency](/learn/tattoo-studio-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't
choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do independent studios have the advantage?

In style and artistry. AI names only a few places and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so an independent with readable styles, clear pricing, and
genuine reviews naming artists and healed results can win the recommendation over a faceless aggregator
listing. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-tattoo-studios) edge an aggregator can't copy — your artists and their
specialties are exactly what a client is searching for.
