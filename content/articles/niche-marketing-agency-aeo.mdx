## Why does a specialization win in AI search?

Because specificity beats generality, and AI names the agency that best fits the exact question. When a
buyer asks "marketing agency that understands SaaS" or "PPC for an e-commerce brand," the engine looks
for a firm that clearly handles that niche's particulars — and a generalist page that says "we serve all
industries" matches nothing in particular. A focused specialization is the
[Originality](/pillars/originality) edge a small agency can own, and exactly the kind of specific,
expert content [citations spread thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands) reward.

## How do I win niche citations?

Give each industry or service you specialize in its own answer-first page covering its specific funnel,
channels, metrics, and results — not a buried line on a generalist page. Show you understand their CAC,
their channels, their sales cycle, reinforced by [reviews and case studies from that
niche](/learn/agency-case-studies-and-results-aeo) and a clear inquiry path. A dedicated 'marketing
agency for SaaS' page gets cited for exactly that query, signals real expertise, and lets you [charge
more and grow](/learn/grow-a-marketing-agency-with-ai-search) — because specialists command higher fees
than generalists.
