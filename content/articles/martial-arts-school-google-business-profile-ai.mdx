## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you teach, and when you're open —
exactly what an engine needs to recommend a dojo. When the assistant decides whom to name for a near-me
query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, programs, and reputation [before
recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, accurate profile makes
you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine unsure and routes the parent
elsewhere.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/martial-arts-school-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement signal, but engines also retrieve and
cite your own [readable program and pricing pages](/learn/martial-arts-school-service-pages-for-ai) for
discipline-level and detailed questions — "kids BJJ near me," "dojo with after-school pickup." You need
both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you, and readable programs for the substance
that wins the discipline query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
