## How do I prepare for each spike?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand turns. Publish or refresh your summer-move
page in late winter, your end-of-month and lease-turnover pages ahead of those recurring waves — before
customers search. Pages need to be [crawled and trusted](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) before
the spike; reacting after summer arrives is too late to be cited in the moment. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a moving calendar.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'how far ahead to book a summer move' or
'best time to move' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the
details each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your
signals. Keep one strong page per topic, sharpen it before each spike, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/moving-company-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the rush hits.
