## Why is ready-to-paint the most valuable painting search?

Because it's the moment money is about to move, and the assistant frames the decision. A homeowner ready
to paint researches cost, timing, and how to choose a contractor before they contact anyone — and the
[AI answer names only a few painters](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people
click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist before you ever hear from them. The painter cited at this stage is the
one the homeowner trusts enough to call first.

## Why does answering cost honestly matter so much?

Because cost is the first thing a homeowner researches and the question most painting sites dodge. A
clear, honest cost range — with what drives it (square footage, prep, number of coats) — earns trust and
citations precisely when intent is highest. Dodging it ("every project is unique, call us") sends the
homeowner to a competitor or a directory that actually answered. Being the painter who explains the money
and the prep honestly is the [Credibility](/pillars/credibility) and [Originality](/pillars/originality)
edge that gets you cited and called — map every one of these to a page in your [questions
library](/learn/painting-questions-customers-ask-ai).
