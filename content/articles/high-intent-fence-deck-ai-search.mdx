## Why is high-intent the most valuable fence and deck search?

Because it's the moment money is about to move, and the assistant frames the whole decision. A
homeowner ready to build researches cost, material, and timeline before they contact anyone — and the
[AI answer names only a few sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found
people click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist before you ever hear from them. The builder cited at this stage is
the one the homeowner trusts enough to call first.

## Why does answering cost honestly matter so much?

Because cost is the first thing a homeowner researches and the question most fence and deck sites
dodge. A clear, honest cost range — per square foot for decks, per linear foot for fences, with the
factors that move it — earns trust and citations precisely when intent is highest. Dodging it ("every
project is unique, call us") sends the homeowner to a competitor or a lead-gen platform that actually
answered. Being the builder who explains the money honestly is the [Credibility](/pillars/credibility)
and [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge that gets you cited and called — map every one of these
questions to a page in your [questions library](/learn/fencing-deck-questions-customers-ask-ai).
