crews.

## Why does AEO matter so much for landscapers?

Because hiring out the yard is a local decision made on the first credible answer. When a homeowner
decides to hire a crew, they ask an assistant and act on what it names — and the [AI answer names only
two or three companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited company is the new equivalent of being the crew a
neighbor recommends — and each account is recurring revenue.

## How do landscapers get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most companies have a slow gallery site and inconsistent listings — so they fail gates one and three
without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the highest-leverage work, and it's the same
foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed site that ranks is the same one that gets [cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds
answering homeowner questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service pages for extraction](/learn/landscaper-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/landscaper-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose
between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do small crews have the advantage?

In specificity and reviews. AI names only a few companies and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a small local crew that answers "how much is weekly
mowing in [city]" and shows real reviews can win the recommendation over a faceless directory. That's
the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-landscapers) edge an
aggregator can't copy — and a recurring account compounds for years.
