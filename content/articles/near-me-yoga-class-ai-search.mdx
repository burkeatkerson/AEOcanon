## Why is 'near me' the highest-value studio search?

Because it's the moment someone decides to join, and it converts into recurring revenue. When a student
asks "best yoga studio near me" or "beginner yoga nearby," they're ready to commit — and the [AI answer
names only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a
link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a studio, owning that moment is the difference between a full
class and an empty one — and every member won keeps paying month after month.

## Why does matching your style matter so much?

Because students search by exactly what they want, and a generic studio matches nothing in particular.
The person searching "beginner-friendly yoga near me" or "reformer Pilates nearby" wants a place that
clearly fits — and if your site states your styles and strengths in plain text, the engine can match
you to that specific, high-intent query. A vague "we offer everything for everyone" page is invisible to
these searches. Be specific about who you're great for, back it with [reviews that prove
it](/learn/yoga-studio-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the students most likely to stay — the
[Originality](/pillars/originality) and [Alignment](/pillars/alignment) edge that fills classes.
