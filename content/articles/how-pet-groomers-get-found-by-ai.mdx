text, they answer the questions pet parents ask, and they're backed by accurate listings and genuine
reviews.** The AI names only a few places, so the groomer who clears all three is the one recommended.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When someone asks for a groomer, the engine pulls the salons it can
read, judges which best fit the request and look well-reviewed, and [names just a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Each step is a filter: if the crawler can't read your
services and pricing, you're out before ranking; if your hours are wrong, you're dropped from near-me;
if your reviews are thin, a competitor gets named. Being found means clearing every filter — and for
groomers, the readable service menu and visible breed/size pricing are often what's missing.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a pet parent — apply, in order.

## Why can't pet parents find my business?

Almost always one specific broken gate — and for groomers it's often the service menu and pricing. If
they're trapped in a [booking widget or image the crawler can't
read](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), the engine doesn't know what you offer or what it costs,
so it can't recommend you for "affordable dog groomer near me" or "groomer that does de-shedding
nearby." Other common breaks: inconsistent hours, or thin reviews. The fix is diagnostic — find the
*highest* broken gate and repair it first. For many groomers, getting services and pricing into plain
text is the highest-leverage move.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking and a map listing help, but AI citation also needs your [services and pricing
in readable text](/learn/pet-grooming-service-pages-for-ai) and genuine reviews. A groomer can show on
a map yet be absent from "best dog groomer near me" answers if the engine can't read the service menu
or the reviews are thin. Clear all three gates and you get [found in
both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
