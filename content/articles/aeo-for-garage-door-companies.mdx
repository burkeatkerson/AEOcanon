repair or new door — by being crawlable, answering the real cost-timing-and-area questions first,
and earning local trust through reviews and warranties.** The reward is the urgent repair and the
install that used to go to a directory.

## Why does AEO matter so much for garage door companies?

Because garage door problems are urgent, safety-sensitive, and decided on the first credible
answer. When a spring snaps or a car's trapped inside, the homeowner asks an assistant and acts
immediately — and the [AI answer names only two or three
sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of links. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so when a homeowner needs help now the answer *is* the front door. Being the cited company is the
new equivalent of being the first call.

## How do garage door companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most garage door companies have a slow site and a reputation that's inconsistent across listings —
so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the
highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that
[ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast, locally-trusted site
that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering questions
[directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service pages for
extraction](/learn/garage-door-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/garage-door-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on. You don't
choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local garage door companies have the advantage?

In specificity and trust. AI names only a few sources and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a trusted local company that answers "how much to
replace a broken garage door spring in [city]" better than a national directory can take the
recommendation. The directory is generic and faceless; you're specific, local, and real. That's
the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-garage-door-companies) edge a big aggregator can't easily copy.
