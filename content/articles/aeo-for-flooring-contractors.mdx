## Why does AEO matter so much for flooring contractors?

Because a floor is a big, researched decision, and the answer is the new front door. A homeowner
planning to replace their flooring asks an assistant long before they call anyone — and the [AI
answer names only two or three sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of links.
Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist before you ever hear from them. Being the cited installer is the
new equivalent of being the trusted name a neighbor recommends.

## How do flooring contractors get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a homeowner — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

    { title: "Be trusted locally (Authority + Credibility)", body: "Consistent name, address, and phone everywhere; license, insurance, and manufacturer certifications stated plainly; real reviews on the platforms engines read; and genuine install proof. This is what decides who gets named." },

Most installers have a slow, gallery-heavy site and a reputation that's inconsistent across listings
— so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is the
highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that [ranks you in
Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast, locally-trusted site
that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering questions
[directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building [service pages for
extraction](/learn/flooring-contractor-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the [reviews and
listings](/learn/flooring-contractor-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on. You
don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local flooring contractors have the advantage?

In specificity and proof. AI names only a few sources and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a trusted local installer who answers "what does it cost
to install LVP in [city]" better than a national platform can take the recommendation. The platform
is generic and faceless; you're specific, local, certified, and real, with real installs to show.
That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-flooring-contractors) edge a big aggregator can't easily copy.
