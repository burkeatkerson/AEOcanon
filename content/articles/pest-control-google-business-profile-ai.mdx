## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of who and where you are — exactly what an
engine needs to recommend a local pest control company. When the assistant decides whom to
name for a near-me or urgent query, it leans on the profile to verify your identity, service
area, hours, and reputation [before recommending
you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete, accurate profile makes
you an easy, safe recommendation; a thin or stale one leaves the engine unsure and routes the
call elsewhere.

Everything an engine checks before vouching for you.

The reviews on your profile do double duty — they're a top input to [whether AI recommends
you](/learn/pest-control-reviews-ai-recommendations) at all.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and trust signal, but engines
also retrieve and cite your [actual service pages](/learn/pest-control-service-pages-for-ai)
for the detailed questions a profile can't answer — what bed bug treatment costs, whether
it's pet-safe, how termite control works. You need both: a complete profile so the engine can
confidently place and trust you, and answer-first pages for the substance that wins the
detailed query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
