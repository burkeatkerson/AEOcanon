## Why is the fall gutter rush the biggest AEO lever?

Because most homeowners book gutter cleaning in fall before winter, and the cited company catches that
wave. Late summer and fall bring a surge of "gutter cleaning near me" and "when should I clean my
gutters" searches as leaves fall; spring brings window cleaning. The company whose answer is already
published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the surge hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo)
— while competitors scramble. It's the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a seasonal
calendar, and the single biggest chance to fill your route.

## How do I prepare for each season?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand spikes.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'fall gutter cleaning' or 'spring window
cleaning' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the dates and
pricing each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits
your signals. Keep one strong page per season, sharpen it before each surge, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/window-gutter-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the rush hits.
