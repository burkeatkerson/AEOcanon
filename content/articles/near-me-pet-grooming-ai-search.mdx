## Why is 'near me' the highest-value grooming search?

Because it's the moment someone decides to book, and it converts into recurring revenue. When a pet
parent asks "best dog groomer near me" or "groomer for anxious dogs nearby," they're ready to commit —
and the [AI answer names only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found
people click a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a groomer, owning that moment is the difference between a full
calendar and a quiet one — and every client won rebooks every few weeks.

## Why does matching your specialty matter so much?

Because pet parents search by exactly what their pet needs, and a generic groomer matches nothing in
particular. The person searching "doodle groomer near me" or "groomer for anxious dogs nearby" wants a
place that clearly fits — and if your site states your specialties in plain text, the engine can match
you to that specific, high-intent query. A vague "all breeds welcome" page is invisible to these
searches. Be specific about who and what you're great with, back it with [reviews that prove
it](/learn/pet-grooming-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the clients most likely to rebook — the
[Originality](/pillars/originality) and [Alignment](/pillars/alignment) edge that fills your calendar.
