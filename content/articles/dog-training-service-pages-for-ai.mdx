## Why are your programs the most important pages?

Because what you offer is what owners search for — and a citation goes to the page the engine can read.
When someone asks "puppy training near me" or "board-and-train for a reactive dog nearby," the engine
matches the query against offerings it can [actually parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site). If
your programs and pricing live only inside a booking widget, the engine doesn't know what you offer, so
you're invisible for those program-level queries — the most valuable, highest-intent searches there
are. Readable offerings turn your whole business into citable answers.

## What makes a program page citable?

Readable text, organized the way owners think — programs and pricing in plain language.

## Why describe programs and methods, not just list them?

Because description is the language of fit and trust. Owners ask AI for "a positive-reinforcement
trainer near me" or "board-and-train for a leash-reactive dog," and a bare program list gives the engine
little to match. A sentence describing each program — who it's for, what method you use, what results to
expect — gives the engine the [extractable detail](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage) that ties you
to those specific, high-intent searches, and mirrors the
[reviews](/learn/dog-training-reviews-ai-recommendations) clients write about your trainers and results.
A readable, described set of programs and pricing is the foundation every other dog training AEO move
builds on.
