## Why does AEO matter so much for DJs?

Because event bookings are high-value, planned, and made on the first good answer. When someone asks
"who can DJ my wedding," they act on what the assistant names — and the [AI answer names only two or
three businesses](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings. Pew Research [found
people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the decision. Being the cited DJ is the new equivalent of being the name every
venue coordinator passes along — and it sends an inquiry that books directly, not a lead resold to four competitors.

## How do DJs get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a client — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most DJs fail gate one without knowing it — pricing and packages are a PDF or hidden behind a
check-availability form, invisible to the engine. Fixing that, plus accurate listings and reviews, is the
highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that [ranks you in
Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast, crawlable,
well-reviewed DJ with readable packages that ranks is the same one that gets
[cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds putting your pricing and answers in [plain
text](/learn/answer-first-sentence), [structured data](/learn/dj-schema-markup) for your service area and
offerings, and the [review consistency](/learn/dj-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines
lean on. You don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do independent DJs have the advantage?

In specificity and authenticity. AI names only a few businesses and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so an independent DJ with readable packages, clear pricing, and
genuine reviews that name events can win the recommendation over an agency or a faceless gig-site listing.
That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-djs) edge
a platform can't copy — and every direct inquiry skips the lead fee.
