## Why are your lessons the binding constraint?

Because [access is the first gate](/pillars/access), and for a coach the lesson menu and pricing are
where you most often fail it. Students search for "beginner golf lessons near me" or "affordable tennis
coach," but if your lessons live inside a [booking widget the crawler can't
read](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), the engine doesn't know what you teach, so it can't
recommend you. Add a slow build or a homepage that's all images, and even your basics are invisible.
That's not a content problem you can write around; it's a foundation problem.

## How do I tell if my site is hurting me?

Run two quick tests, and look for the structural gaps.

If your lessons are widget-trapped, your pricing is hidden, or the page is slow, the site is working
against you. A fast site with [readable lessons and pricing](/learn/sports-instructor-service-pages-for-ai)
and [clean schema](/learn/sports-instructor-schema-markup) is what makes everything else possible.

## Can't I just keep my booking widget?

Keep the widget for booking — but you also need your lessons, packages, and pricing in readable HTML
text on the page. Because the widget is usually invisible to crawlers, relying on it alone hides exactly
the lessons students search for. Add readable text alongside it, get the access layer right —
[server-rendered](/learn/server-side-vs-client-side-rendering), fast, with readable lessons — and the
rest of your coaching AEO finally has something to build on.
