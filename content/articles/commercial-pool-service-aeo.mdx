## How is commercial pool AEO different?

The intent is a researched, compliance-driven decision, not a homeowner job. A property manager, HOA
board, or hotel evaluates carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before they call.
They're researching health-code requirements, certifications, and whether you can keep a high-traffic
pool compliant and safe. Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the
company whose pages answer those questions clearly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a recurring contract worth far more than a
single residential account.

## How do I win commercial citations?

Give commercial pool maintenance its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage. Publish
your scope, compliance practices, certifications, insurance, and experience in readable text, with a
clear inquiry path. Most pool companies either omit this or hide it, so a clear, dedicated page is an
easy way to get cited for valuable, recurring commercial contracts — the kind that anchor a route and
compound your [growth](/learn/grow-a-pool-service-business-with-ai-search).
