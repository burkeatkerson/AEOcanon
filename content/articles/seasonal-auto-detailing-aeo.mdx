## How do I prepare for each spike?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand turns. Publish or refresh your
ceramic-and-protection page in late winter, your winter-prep page in early fall — ahead of when owners
search. Pages need to be [crawled and trusted](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) before the
spike; reacting mid-season is too late to be cited in the moment. It's the [Freshness
pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a detailing calendar.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'is ceramic coating worth it' or
'winter salt protection' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the
details each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits
your signals. Keep one strong page per topic, sharpen it before each spike, and link it to your [package
pages](/learn/auto-detailing-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the season turns.
