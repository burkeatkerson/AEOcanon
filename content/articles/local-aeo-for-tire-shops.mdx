## What makes a tire shop 'local' to an engine?

Confidence about where you are and whether you're open. An answer engine recommends a tire shop for
"near me" or "open now" only when it can [place you with
certainty](/learn/how-ai-chooses-local-business-to-recommend) — right area, open now. That certainty
comes from signals that all agree: matching name-address-phone, accurate hours, a complete profile, and
reviews. When the signals agree, you're an easy recommendation; when your hours conflict, the engine
sends the driver elsewhere.

This is the [near-me ranking](/learn/how-to-rank-in-ai-for-near-me-searches) discipline applied to the
trade — and accurate hours are where most shops quietly lose.

## Why do accurate hours matter so much?

Because 'tire shop open now near me' is one of the most common urgent tire queries, and the engine
answers it straight from your hours. Accurate, consistent hours mean you get recommended when a driver
with a flat needs you; wrong or inconsistent hours mean you're dropped — or you send someone to a closed
door and earn a bad review. Keep hours accurate and identical everywhere, including holidays, and you
remove the single most fixable reason a tire shop gets skipped. It's the same [NAP
consistency](/learn/nap-consistency) discipline, with the clock attached.
