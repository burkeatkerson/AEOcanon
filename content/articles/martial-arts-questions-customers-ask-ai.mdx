benefits ('confidence', 'focus', 'self-defense').** Mapping each to readable content that answers it is
the core of a dojo AEO content plan.

## How do I find the exact questions?

Listen where parents already ask. Note what people ask at the front desk and on trial visits, read your
reviews and the questions on your Google profile, scan local parenting and martial arts forums and
groups, and [prompt the assistants directly](/learn/how-to-find-questions-ai-users-ask) on your
disciplines and area to see the follow-ups they surface. Capture the *natural wording* — "is there a
karate place near me that's good for a 5-year-old" beats "youth martial arts instruction facility" —
because engines match the parent's phrasing. Then prioritize by what genuinely fits your school.

## Should I publish pricing if competitors hide it?

Yes — and it's an edge, not a risk. Parents constantly ask AI "how much are martial arts classes near
me," and a clear starting price or tuition range in readable text lets the engine recommend you for those
high-intent cost searches — while competitors who bury pricing in a widget get skipped. Transparency also
builds trust with a budget-conscious parent before they ever visit. It's pure
[Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question, answered where the engine and the parent can read it.
Map every bucket to readable content and you've built the plan that gets a dojo cited.
