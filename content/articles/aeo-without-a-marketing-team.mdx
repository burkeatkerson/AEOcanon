clearly, keeping your details consistent, earning reviews, and joining the

not a marketing department. Here's the lean routine.

## Why solo owners have a real advantage

Solo owners have an advantage hiding in plain sight: you *are* the expert, and AI
rewards genuine, specific expertise. You answer your customers' questions out loud
every day — AEO is mostly writing those answers down clearly so AI can quote them.
You don't need a content team to produce generic articles; you need to capture the
real, first-hand answers only you can give. That authenticity is exactly what
engines favor, and it's something a big competitor's outsourced content often lacks.

## What to do once (the setup)

Spend your first few sessions on one-time setup, then you're into the light routine:

<Steps
  items={[
    { title: "Check your site is readable", body: "Confirm AI tools aren't blocked and your text loads plainly. A quick check with your site builder's help docs covers it." },
    { title: "Claim your profiles", body: "Google Business Profile and your main industry directories — claim, verify, and fill them out fully." },
    { title: "Make your details consistent", body: "Name, address, and phone identical everywhere. Fix mismatches once." },
    { title: "List your real questions", body: "Write down the 15–20 questions customers actually ask you. This is your content plan." },
  ]}
/>

These are the [Foundations](/courses/aeo-foundations) basics, done once.

## The weekly routine (2–4 hours)

Then settle into a small, repeatable routine you can keep up alone:

<Steps
  items={[
    { title: "Write one good answer (about an hour)", body: "Take one question from your list and write a clear page — answer in the first sentence, one real fact or example, plain language." },
    { title: "Do a little off-site participation (about an hour)", body: "Answer a question or two genuinely in a relevant Reddit thread or forum, or ask a happy customer for a review." },
    { title: "Tidy one detail (15 minutes)", body: "Fix one inconsistency, update one profile, or add one missing fact somewhere you appear." },
  ]}
/>

Over a few months, one good answer a week becomes a library of pages AI can quote,
and steady participation becomes real off-site presence.

## Why communities are your highest-leverage free move

Communities are your best free lever because AI leans on them heavily — Reddit is
the single most-cited domain across AI engines, and genuine answers there get read
by both people and engines. You don't need ad spend or a PR agency; you need to
show up where your topic is discussed and be authentically helpful. One thoughtful
answer a week, in the right place, compounds. The honest version of this is in [do I
need to be on Reddit to get cited by AI?](/learn/reddit-ai-citations) — and the rule
is simple: contribute, don't spam.

## You don't need to be a writer

Don't worry about polished prose — plain, direct answers beat clever writing for AI.
State the answer, back it with a real fact or example, and stop. You already explain
these things to customers; write them down the same way. Clear and honest is the
whole craft, and it's covered simply in [how to write an answer-first
sentence](/learn/answer-first-sentence).
