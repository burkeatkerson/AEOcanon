**AI search visitors are warm and pre-qualified, so converting them is about
continuity — confirm the claim that brought them, add the depth the answer couldn't,
remove friction, and make the next step obvious.** They already convert ~23x organic
([Ahrefs](/learn/does-ai-search-send-traffic)); your job is not to break that
momentum with a generic experience.

## What's the core principle?

The core principle is continuity: pick up exactly where the AI answer left off. A
visitor who clicks through has already been sold on the idea by the answer and chosen
your page to go deeper — so the worst thing you can do is treat them like a stranger
and re-explain the basics. Instead, confirm the specific point that brought them
("Yes — here's how our plan handles X"), then add what the answer couldn't: detail,
proof, specifics, and the path to act. This is the
[AI visitor journey](/learn/ai-visitor-journey) made actionable — your page is step
four of five, not step one.

## What kills conversion for these visitors?

What kills conversion is breaking continuity and adding friction. Sending a warm,
specific visitor to a generic homepage, re-introducing the topic they already
understand, burying the next step, or gating the page behind a form or a slow load —
each makes a pre-qualified visitor restart or bounce. The failure mode here is *not*
weak persuasion (the AI already persuaded them); it's a confusing or generic
experience that squanders the qualification. And because [there are fewer of these
high-value visitors](/learn/how-ai-referral-traffic-behaves), every one lost to a
poor experience is expensive.

## Where should AI visitors land?

AI visitors should land on a specific page that matches the answer's intent, not a
catch-all homepage. They came for a particular question or recommendation, so they
should arrive where that's confirmed and extended with a clear next step for their
stage. You often can't control the exact landing URL an AI sends, so the practical
move is to make your most-cited pages double as strong continuation-and-conversion
experiences — covered in [landing pages for AI
traffic](/learn/landing-pages-for-ai-traffic).
