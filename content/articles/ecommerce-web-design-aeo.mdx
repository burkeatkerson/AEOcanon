## Why does an ecommerce niche win in AI search?

Because specificity beats generality, and AI names the studio that best fits the exact question. When an
owner asks "Shopify designer who understands subscriptions" or "how much does an online store cost," the
engine looks for a studio that clearly handles a store's particulars — catalogs, checkout, payments,
tax, shipping — and a generalist page that says "we build all kinds of websites" matches nothing in
particular. A focused ecommerce niche is the [Originality](/pillars/originality) edge a small studio can
own, and exactly the kind of specific, expert content [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands) reward.
