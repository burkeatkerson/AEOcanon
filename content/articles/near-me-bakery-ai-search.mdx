## Why is 'near me' the highest-value bakery search?

Because it's the moment of decision, and it converts immediately. When someone asks "best custom cakes
near me" or "gluten-free bakery open now," they're choosing where to order today — and the [AI answer
names only a few places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a
link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the order. For a bakery, owning that moment is the difference between a sold-out case
and an empty one — and it's where the high-margin custom orders start.

## What do customers ask AI when choosing a bakery?

In-the-moment and specialty questions — and you should own the ones that fit your bakery.

## Why is 'open now' the make-or-break signal?

Because a large share of bakery searches are in-the-moment, and the engine answers 'open now' straight
from your hours. Accurate, consistent hours mean you get recommended when a customer is deciding; wrong
or inconsistent hours mean you're dropped from the answer — or you send someone to a closed door and
earn a bad review. Keeping hours current everywhere, including the holidays when bakeries peak, is the
highest-leverage move for winning the in-the-moment order, and it's the same [local
certainty](/learn/local-aeo-for-bakeries) that wins every near-me query.
