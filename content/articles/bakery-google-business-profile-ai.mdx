## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you make, and whether you're open
— exactly what an engine needs to recommend a bakery. When the assistant decides whom to name for a
near-me or 'open now' query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, products, and
reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete,
accurate profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine
unsure — or sends a customer to a closed door.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and 'open now' signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [readable product list](/learn/bakery-menu-pages-for-ai) and pages for
product-level and detailed questions — "gluten-free wedding cake near me," "how far ahead to order a
custom cake." You need both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you and confirm you're
open, and a readable product list and custom-order page for the substance that wins the discovery query.
Treat them as one system, kept [consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
