## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you do, and whether you're open —
exactly what an engine needs to recommend a tire shop. When the assistant decides whom to name for a
near-me or 'open now' query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, services, and
reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete,
accurate profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine
unsure — or sends a driver to a closed door.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and 'open now' signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [service pages](/learn/tire-shop-service-pages-for-ai) for the detailed
questions a profile can't answer — what 4 tires cost, which brands you carry, alignment pricing. You need
both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you and confirm you're open, and answer-first
pages for the substance that wins the detailed query. Treat them as one system, kept [consistent
everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
