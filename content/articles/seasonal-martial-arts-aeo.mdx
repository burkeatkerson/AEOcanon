## Why does seasonality matter for martial arts schools?

Because enrollment surges in predictable waves, and the cited dojo catches them. Fall brings the
back-to-school wave, the biggest window for kids and after-school programs; January brings the resolution
wave of adults and families; spring brings summer-camp searches. The dojo whose answer is already
published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the wave
hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors are still updating their site. It's
the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into an enrollment calendar, and the difference between
a packed program and a quiet one.

## How do I prepare for each wave?

Build the content while it's calm, so it's ready when families are ready.

## Should I make a new page each year for camp or January?

No — update one durable evergreen page per theme. A persistent 'summer martial arts camp' or 'kids
after-school program' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh dates
and details each year, which beats spinning up a throwaway 'Summer 2026' page that starts from zero and
splits your signals. Keep one strong page per theme that answers the season's questions, sharpen it
before each wave, and link it to your [program and pricing
pages](/learn/martial-arts-school-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats
scrambling when the enrollment wave hits.
