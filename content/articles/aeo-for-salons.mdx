## Why does AEO matter so much for salons?

Because choosing a salon or barber is a local, trust-heavy decision, and the answer is the new front
door. When someone needs a cut or color, they ask an assistant and act on what it names — and the [AI
answer names only two or three places](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a page of listings.
Pew Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer shapes the shortlist. Being the cited salon is the new equivalent of being the chair
everyone recommends — and each client is recurring revenue, not a one-time sale.

## How do salons get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a client — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

    { title: "Answer the real question (Alignment + Extractability)", body: "Plain-text answers to 'how much is balayage', 'do you do fades', 'do you take walk-ins', 'is there parking', and 'where are you'." },
    { title: "Be trusted (Authority + Credibility)", body: "Consistent name, address, and phone everywhere; a complete Google Business Profile; and genuine, recent reviews that name stylists, services, and results. This is what decides who gets named." },

Most salons fail gate one without knowing it — the menu and prices live in a booking widget the engine
can't read. Fixing that, plus accurate listings and reviews, is the highest-leverage work, and it's the
same foundation that [ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the local SEO and GEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. The fast,
crawlable, well-reviewed salon with a readable menu and pricing that ranks is the same one that gets
[cited](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo). AEO adds putting your menu and answers in [plain
text](/learn/answer-first-sentence), [structured data](/learn/salon-schema-markup), and the [review
consistency](/learn/salon-reviews-ai-recommendations) answer engines lean on. You don't choose between
them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do independent salons have the advantage?

In specialty and personality. AI names only a few places and [citations spread
thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so an independent with a readable menu, clear pricing, and
genuine reviews naming stylists and results can win the recommendation over a faceless chain or
marketplace listing. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-salons) edge a chain can't copy — your balayage specialist, your barber,
and your regulars are exactly what a client is looking for.
