## Why does Google Business Profile matter for AI search?

Because it's a structured, trusted confirmation of where you are, what you do, and whether you can reach
the driver — exactly what an engine needs to recommend an auto glass shop. When the assistant decides whom
to name for a near-me or mobile query, it leans on the profile to verify your location, hours, services,
and reputation [before recommending you](/learn/does-google-business-profile-help-ai-search). A complete,
accurate profile makes you an easy recommendation; wrong hours or a thin profile leaves the engine
unsure — or sends a driver to a closed door.

## Does GBP replace a good website?

No — it's half the picture. The profile is a powerful placement and availability signal, but engines also
retrieve and cite your own [service pages](/learn/auto-glass-shop-service-pages-for-ai) for the detailed
questions a profile can't answer — what a replacement costs, whether insurance covers it, ADAS calibration,
OEM vs aftermarket. You need both: a complete, accurate profile so the engine can place you, and
answer-first pages for the substance that wins the detailed query. Treat them as one system, kept
[consistent everywhere](/learn/nap-consistency).
