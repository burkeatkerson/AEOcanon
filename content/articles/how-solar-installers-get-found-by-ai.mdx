**Solar installers get found by AI search when their site is crawlable, answers the real cost and
payback questions homeowners ask, and is backed by consistent local trust signals like reviews,
certifications, and install proof.** The AI retrieves and ranks the few sources that clear all three
— so the installer who does is the one named in the answer.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When someone asks about going solar or for an installer, the engine
pulls the sources it can read, judges which best answer the question and look trustworthy, and
[names just a few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) in the answer. Each step is a filter: if a
crawler can't fetch your page, you're out before ranking; if your page dodges the cost or payback
question, you're out at ranking; if your trust signals are thin, a competitor gets named instead.
Being found means clearing every filter.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a homeowner — apply, in order.

## Why can't customers find my solar business?

Almost always one specific broken gate — not bad luck. Your site is an animation-heavy shell that
[crawlers see as nearly empty](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), or it's slow; your pages bury
the cost and payback answer under company history; or your reviews and listings are thin and
inconsistent. The fix is diagnostic: find the *highest* broken gate and repair it first, because a
great reputation can't rescue a site bots can't read, and a fast site can't rescue a page that never
answers "is solar worth it here". This is the same
[why-isn't-my-business-showing-up](/learn/why-isnt-my-local-business-showing-up-in-ai) logic, applied
to the trade.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking proves Google can read and rank you — a good sign for the first gate — but AI
citation also needs an [extractable, answer-first page](/learn/answer-first-sentence) and trusted
local signals. An installer can rank on page one yet be absent from AI answers if the payback answer
is buried three paragraphs down or off-site trust is thin. The overlap is real but not total: clear
all three gates and you get [found in both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
