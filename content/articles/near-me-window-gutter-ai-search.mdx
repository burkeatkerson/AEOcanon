## Why is near-me the highest-value window & gutter search?

Because it's the moment a homeowner decides to book — and sometimes it's urgent. When someone asks
"gutter cleaning near me" or "overflowing gutters who to call," they're ready to act, and overflowing
gutters before heavy rain put their home at risk — so they act fast. The [AI answer names only a few
companies](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a link just 8% of
the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the booking. For a window & gutter company, owning that moment fills the route — and
an urgent job often becomes a recurring seasonal customer.

## Why does publishing pricing and availability help so much?

Because homeowners ask AI "how much to clean gutters near me," and a clear price or range in readable
text lets the engine recommend you for those high-intent searches — while competitors who hide pricing
get skipped. For urgent overflowing-gutter searches, accurate availability and a clear service area let
the engine confirm you can help now. Both are pure [Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question,
answered where the engine and the homeowner can read it. Be specific, back it with
[reviews](/learn/window-gutter-reviews-ai-recommendations), and you win the near-me job that turns into a
recurring customer.
