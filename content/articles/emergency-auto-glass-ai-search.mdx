## Why is urgent intent the highest-value auto glass search?

Because it converts on the spot. When a windshield shatters in a break-in or a crack spreads across the
driver's view, the driver isn't comparison-shopping — they ask an assistant, get an answer, and call the
first credible name. There's no page two. Pew Research [found people click a link just 8% of the time when
an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the decision. For an auto glass shop, owning that moment fills the schedule — and an
emergency replacement done today often becomes the customer's shop for every future job.

## Why do hours and coverage decide the urgent booking?

Because 'near me now' is a placement question, and the engine answers it from your hours and coverage
area. If your hours conflict between your site and your Google profile, or your mobile area is vague, the
engine can't confidently say you're available *here, now* — so it names a competitor it's sure about. Keep
hours accurate and identical everywhere, state same-day, mobile, and board-up availability plainly, and you
remove the most common reason an auto glass shop loses the urgent booking. This is the same [local
certainty](/learn/local-aeo-for-auto-glass-shops) that wins every near-me query, sharpened by the clock.
