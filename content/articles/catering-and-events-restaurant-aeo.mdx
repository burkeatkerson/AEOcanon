## How is catering and events AEO different?

The intent is planning, not a walk-in. An event planner, office manager, or host booking a birthday
researches carefully — and asks the assistant detailed logistics questions long before they call.
They're researching capacity, pricing, and whether you can handle their group and dietary needs.
Because [AEO works for these higher-consideration decisions](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b), the
restaurant whose pages answer those questions clearly gets [cited during the
research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a high-value booking most restaurants never
even surface for.

## What questions do planners ask?

Logistics-focused and specific — each worth a clear answer on a dedicated page.

## How do I win catering and event citations?

Give catering and private events their own answer-first pages — not a buried line on your homepage.
Publish capacity, pricing structure, sample catering menus, dietary accommodations, and lead time in
readable text, reinforced by your [reviews](/learn/restaurant-reviews-ai-recommendations) and a clear
inquiry path. Most restaurants either omit this entirely or trap it in a PDF, so a clear, dedicated page
is an easy way to get cited for valuable corporate, group, and private bookings — repeat, high-margin
revenue that compounds your [growth](/learn/grow-a-restaurant-with-ai-search).
