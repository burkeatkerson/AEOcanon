## Why do expert quotes matter for AEO?

Because they're independent proof of expertise. A quote in a credible outlet is a
[mention rather than a link](/learn/mentions-vs-backlinks), and it tells engines what
you're known for from a source that isn't you. As those mentions accumulate across
trusted publications, your [entity gets stronger](/learn/does-being-quoted-by-journalists-help-aeo)
and you become a safer name for AI to cite and recommend.

## What makes a reply land?

Credibility, brevity, and speed. Lead with who you are and why you can speak to the
topic, give one sharp quotable line, and send it before the deadline closes. Build a
[credentialed expert profile](/authority/get-mentioned-in-publications) so journalists
can verify you at a glance, and treat this as ongoing [digital PR for
AI](/authority/digital-pr-for-ai) rather than a one-off push.
