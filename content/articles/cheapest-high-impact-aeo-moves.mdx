**The AEO moves with the best return for the least money are free:** put the answer
first, make sure AI can read your site, keep your details consistent, earn reviews
and mentions, and back your claims with evidence. Each is supported by real data —
and each costs effort, not budget.

## 1. Lead with the answer (free, highest return)

Move your answer to the first sentence of every important page. This is the single
best free move, because AI tools favor the top of the page —
[Profound found 44% of AI citations come from the first third](/learn/answer-first-sentence).
Most sites bury their answer under a long intro, so simply leading with it puts you
ahead for the cost of some editing. Do this to your five most important pages this
week.

## 2. Make sure AI can read your site (free)

Check that AI tools aren't blocked and your text loads plainly. If the tools can't
read you, none of your other effort counts. It's a quick check: confirm your
[robots.txt](/glossary/robots-txt) isn't blocking them, and make sure your words show without scripts
running (turn off JavaScript and reload — your content should still be there). The
plain guide is [how to allow AI crawlers in robots.txt](/learn/allow-ai-crawlers-robots-txt)
(and [Google's robots.txt documentation](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/crawling-indexing/robots/intro)).
Most site builders handle this fine, so it's usually verify-and-move-on.

## 3. Keep your details consistent (free)

Make your business name, address, and phone identical everywhere they appear. AI
builds its picture of you by merging mentions from across the web; conflicting
details fragment that picture and make you harder to recommend. Fixing it costs an
afternoon and pays off in recognition — this is [NAP
consistency](/learn/nap-consistency), and it's pure upside.

## 4. Earn reviews and mentions (free)

Ask happy customers for honest reviews, and get genuinely mentioned where your topic
comes up. Being *talked about* beats being *linked to* for AI, and it's cheaper:
[Ahrefs found brand mentions tracked AI visibility far more strongly than backlinks
(0.664 versus 0.218)](/pillars/authority). You don't need a link-building budget —
reviews, community answers, local press, and directory listings all count. One
genuine community answer a week (see [Reddit
citations](/learn/reddit-ai-citations)) is high-leverage and free.

## 5. Add a fact to every claim (free)

Back your important claims with a real number, example, or source — in the same
sentence. Evidence makes your answer safe for AI to repeat: the [Princeton GEO study
found adding quotations and statistics were among the top tactics](/learn/statistics-quotes-citations-aeo)
for raising visibility. "We're fast" becomes "most jobs are done within 48 hours."
It costs nothing but a moment's specificity, and it makes you more quotable.
