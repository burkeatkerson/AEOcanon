## Why are readable arrangements the most important pages?

Because what you offer is what buyers search for — and a citation goes to the page the engine can read.
When someone asks "same-day sympathy flowers near me" or "wedding florist nearby," the engine matches
the query against arrangements and occasions it can [actually parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site).
If your site is all photos with no descriptions, the engine doesn't know what you offer, so you're
invisible for those occasion and delivery queries — the most valuable, highest-intent searches. Readable
arrangements turn your whole shop into citable answers.

## What makes an arrangement page citable?

Readable text, organized the way buyers think.

## Why describe arrangements, not just show them?

Because description is the language of discovery. Buyers ask AI for "a sympathy arrangement" or "what to
send for a new baby," and a wall of photos gives the engine nothing to match. A sentence describing each
arrangement — its style, the flowers, the occasion it suits — gives the engine the [extractable
detail](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage) that ties you to those searches, and mirrors the
[reviews](/learn/florist-reviews-ai-recommendations) buyers write about your work. Readable, described
arrangements and delivery info are the foundation every other florist AEO move builds on.
