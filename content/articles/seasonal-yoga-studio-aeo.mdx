## Why does seasonality matter for studios?

Because motivation — and sign-ups — surge in predictable waves, and the cited studio catches them.
January brings the resolution wave, the biggest sign-up window of the year; the post-holiday reset
sends people looking for a fresh start; summer brings lighter-schedule and summer-body searches. The
studio whose answer is already published, crawled, and trusted [gets cited the moment the wave
hits](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) — while competitors are still updating their site. It's
the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned into a motivation calendar, and the difference between
a packed January and a quiet one.

## How do I prepare for each wave?

Build the content while it's calm, so it's ready when motivation spikes.

## Should I make a new 'New Year' page each year?

No — update one durable evergreen page. A persistent 'beginner-friendly yoga' or 'start your yoga
journey' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh it each year,
which beats spinning up a throwaway 'New Year 2026' page that starts from zero and splits your signals.
Keep one strong page that answers the beginner and intro-offer questions, sharpen it before each wave,
and link it to your [class and pricing pages](/learn/yoga-studio-service-pages-for-ai). One durable
page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling when the resolution wave hits.
