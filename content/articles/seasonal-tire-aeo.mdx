## How do I prepare for each spike?

Build the content while it's quiet, so it's ready when demand turns. Publish or refresh your winter-tire
page in late summer, your spring-swap page in late winter — ahead of when drivers search. Pages need to
be [crawled and trusted](/learn/does-seasonal-content-work-for-aeo) before the spike; reacting after the
first snow is too late to be cited in the moment. It's the [Freshness pillar](/pillars/freshness) turned
into a tire calendar.

## Should I make a new page each year?

No — update one durable page per seasonal topic. A persistent 'when to switch to winter tires' or 'tire
storage' page [accumulates authority](/learn/business-case-for-aeo) while you refresh the details each
year, which beats spinning up a throwaway page annually that starts from zero and splits your signals.
Keep one strong page per topic, sharpen it before each spike, and link it to your [service
pages](/learn/tire-shop-service-pages-for-ai). One durable page, refreshed and ready, beats scrambling
when the changeover hits.
