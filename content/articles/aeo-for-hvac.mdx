## Why does AEO matter so much for HVAC?

Because HVAC is seasonal, often urgent, and decided on the first credible answer. When the
AC fails in a heat wave, the customer asks an assistant and acts immediately — and the
[AI answer names only two or three sources](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations), not a
page of links. Pew Research
[found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so when comfort can't wait the answer *is* the front door. Being the cited contractor is
the new equivalent of being the first call.

## How do HVAC companies get recommended by AI?

By clearing the three gates a customer — and an engine — judge you on, in order.

Most HVAC companies have a slow site and a reputation that's inconsistent across listings —
so they fail gates one and three without realizing the contest happened. Fixing those is
the highest-leverage work, and it's the same foundation that
[ranks you in Google](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together) too.

## Isn't this just SEO?

It's mostly the SEO you should already be doing, pointed at a new surface. Roughly
[70 to 80 percent overlaps](/learn/is-aeo-replacing-seo): the crawlable, fast,
locally-trusted site that ranks is the same one that gets cited. AEO adds answering
questions [directly and answer-first](/learn/answer-first-sentence), building
[service pages for extraction](/learn/hvac-service-pages-for-ai), and tightening the
[reviews and listings](/learn/hvac-reviews-ai-recommendations) that answer engines lean on.
You don't choose between them — you run one program that wins both.

## Where do local HVAC contractors have the advantage?

In specificity and trust. AI names only a few sources and
[citations spread thin](/learn/is-aeo-only-for-big-brands), so a focused local contractor
who answers "how much does a new AC cost in [city]" better than a national directory can
take the recommendation. The directory is generic and faceless; you're specific, local,
certified, and real. That's the [Originality](/pillars/originality) and [local
authority](/learn/local-aeo-for-hvac) edge a big aggregator can't easily copy — and for a
trust-driven trade, verifiable certifications are proof a directory listing can't show.
