## How is commercial landscaping AEO different?

The intent is a researched B2B decision, not a homeowner job. A property manager or HOA board
evaluates carefully — and asks the assistant detailed questions long before they call. They're
researching scope, capacity, insurance, and whether you can keep their property pristine year-round.
Because [AEO works for B2B](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b) exactly here, the company whose pages answer
those questions clearly gets [cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and
wins a recurring contract worth far more than a single residential job.

## How do I win commercial citations?

Give commercial grounds maintenance its own answer-first page — not a buried line on your homepage.
Publish your scope, capacity, insurance, snow management, and references in readable text, with a clear
inquiry path. Most landscapers either omit this or hide it in a gallery, so a clear, dedicated page is
an easy way to get cited for valuable, recurring commercial contracts — the kind that anchor a route and
compound your [growth](/learn/grow-a-landscaping-business-with-ai-search).
