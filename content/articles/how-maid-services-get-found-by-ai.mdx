**Maid services get found by AI search when their site is crawlable, answers the real questions clients
ask, and is backed by consistent listings and genuine reviews.** The AI names only a few services, so the
maid service that clears all three is the one recommended instead of a resold lead.

## What does an AI engine actually do to find you?

It retrieves, ranks, and names. When a client asks for a maid service, the engine pulls the companies it
can read, judges which best answer the question and look trustworthy, and [names just a
few](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Each step is a filter: if a crawler can't fetch your page,
you're out before ranking; if your page doesn't answer the cost or trust question, you're out at ranking;
if your reviews are thin, a competitor gets named. Being found means clearing every filter.

## What are the three gates?

The same three an engine — and a client — apply, in order.

## Why can't clients find my maid service?

Almost always one specific broken gate — and for maid services it's often the widget-only site. If your
site is mostly a booking widget with little [readable text the crawler can
parse](/learn/check-ai-crawlers-can-read-site), the engine doesn't know what you offer or what it costs,
so it can't recommend you for "recurring maid service near me" or "move-out cleaning in [town]." Other
common breaks: inconsistent listings, or thin reviews. The fix is diagnostic — find the *highest* broken
gate and repair it first. For most maid services, putting services, pricing, and trust signals in real
text is the highest-leverage move.

## Does ranking in Google mean AI can find me?

Not on its own. Ranking proves Google can read and rank you — a good sign for the first gate — but AI
citation also needs an [extractable, answer-first page](/learn/answer-first-sentence) and genuine
reviews. A maid service can rank yet be absent from AI answers if the answer is buried or reviews are
thin. The overlap is real but not total: clear all three gates and you get [found in
both](/learn/how-aeo-and-seo-work-together).
