## Why is wedding catering the highest-value search to win?

Because it's the biggest, most-researched catering decision a client makes — and it converts to a large,
high-margin booking. When a couple asks "wedding catering cost per person near me" or "caterer for 120
guests," they're choosing who to trust with one of the biggest days of their life — and the [AI answer
names only a few businesses](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations). Pew Research [found people click a
link just 8% of the time when an AI summary
appears](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/),
so the answer *is* the shortlist. For a caterer, owning that moment is the difference between a booked
wedding season and a quiet one.

## Why publish pricing and venue experience, not just photos?

Because detail is the language of the wedding decision. A gallery looks nice, but a couple asks AI for the
"cost per person," "whether you've worked our venue," and "if you can handle our guest count and dietary
needs" — and photos give the engine nothing to match. Stating your per-head ranges, guest-count
capability, tasting process, and venue experience gives the engine the [extractable
detail](/learn/nine-properties-citable-passage) that ties you to those exact searches, pre-qualifies the
inquiry, and mirrors the [wedding reviews](/learn/caterer-reviews-ai-recommendations) couples write.
Readable, detailed wedding pages are how you win the highest-value catering booking — direct, with no lead
fee.
