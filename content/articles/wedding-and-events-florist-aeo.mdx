## How is wedding and event AEO different?

The intent is months of planning, not a same-day order. A couple or planner researches carefully — and
asks the assistant detailed questions long before they book. They're researching budgets, styles, and
whether you're available and right for their vision. Because [AEO works for these higher-consideration
decisions](/learn/does-aeo-work-for-b2b), the florist whose pages answer those questions clearly gets
[cited during the research](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) — and wins a high-value, high-margin
booking most florists never even surface for.

## What questions do couples and planners ask?

Planning-stage, specific questions — each worth a clear page.

These reward [evidenced, expert content](/learn/how-to-show-expertise-to-ai) and a described portfolio,
not a bare gallery.

## How do I win wedding and event citations?

Give weddings and events their own answer-first pages — not a buried gallery. Publish budget guidance,
styles, seasonal flowers, and your consultation process in readable text, with a described portfolio of
past work and a clear inquiry path. Most florists hide all of this in photos, so a clear, dedicated page
is an easy way to get cited for valuable, high-margin event bookings — the kind that compound your
[growth](/learn/grow-a-flower-shop-with-ai-search) far beyond a single arrangement.
