## How do I find the exact questions?

Listen where prospects already ask. Note what people ask at the front desk and on tours, read your
reviews and the questions on your Google profile, scan local fitness forums and groups, and [prompt the
assistants directly](/learn/how-to-find-questions-ai-users-ask) on your niche and area to see the
follow-ups they surface. Capture the *natural wording* — "is there a gym near me that's good for total
beginners" beats "novice-oriented fitness facility" — because engines match the prospect's phrasing.
Then prioritize by what genuinely fits your gym.

## Should I publish pricing if competitors hide it?

Yes — and it's an edge, not a risk. Prospects constantly ask AI "how much is a gym near me," and a
clear starting price or range in readable text lets the engine recommend you for those high-intent
cost searches — while competitors who bury pricing in a widget get skipped. Transparency also builds
trust with a cost-conscious prospect before they ever visit. It's pure
[Alignment](/pillars/alignment): the real question, answered where the engine and the prospect can read
it. Map every bucket to readable content and you've built the plan that gets a gym cited.
