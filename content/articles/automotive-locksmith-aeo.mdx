## Why is automotive key work the growth opportunity for locksmiths?

Because it's high-margin demand that's growing, and most of it routes through an AI question first.
Modern cars use transponder keys, smart keys, and fobs that are expensive and slow to replace at the
dealer — and drivers increasingly search for a local locksmith who can cut and program them for less,
often on the spot. It's profitable work, the field is under-served by genuine local pros, and the
buyers are researching online before they call. That makes it a textbook AEO opportunity: be the
locksmith the assistant [names in the answer](/learn/how-ai-engines-choose-citations) and you capture
a steady stream of these jobs before competitors even know they were searched.
