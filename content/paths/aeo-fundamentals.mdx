## What you'll learn

This is the foundational course of the AEO school: everything you need to make
your content the source AI answer engines read, trust, and quote. It assumes no
prior AEO knowledge — just a willingness to write clearly and ship.

You'll start with the framework as a whole, then take one lesson per pillar, in
the exact order you should apply them. The pillars are a cascade: each lesson
builds on the one before it, so working through them in sequence is the point.

## How the course works

The course opens with what AEO is and an overview of the eight-pillar Canon and
its three layers. From there, each lesson goes deep on a single pillar — the
principle, the evidence behind it, and the specific moves to apply it to your own
site. Every lesson is a standalone article that also links back here, so you can
pause and resume without losing the thread.

By the end you'll be able to diagnose exactly why an answer engine recommends a
competitor instead of you — and fix it, in the right order.
