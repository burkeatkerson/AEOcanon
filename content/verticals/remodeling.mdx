the journey now starts with a question to an AI, not a scroll through ten links. *"How much does a
kitchen remodel cost?"* *"How long does a bathroom renovation take?"* *"Who's a trusted remodeler
near me?"* The assistant answers and names two or three sources, and the homeowner reaches out to
the first one. For most remodelers, that name is a national lead-gen platform that then sells your
own neighborhood's renovation back to you as a shared lead. **This library is about flipping that:
becoming the remodeler AI recommends, so you stop renting leads and start owning your pipeline.**

## Why AEO is the highest-leverage move in remodeling

Because the economics are brutal and the answer is the new front door. Remodelers spend a fortune
on purchased leads that are sold to several competitors at once, then compete on price for a job
they paid to bid on. Meanwhile the homeowner who asked an assistant *"best kitchen remodeler near
me"* already got a recommendation — and it wasn't you. When the surface was a page of links, you
could buy your way on. Now the surface is a single synthesized answer that names a few trusted
sources, and [clicks to everything else collapse](/learn/ai-search-vs-traditional-search): Pew
Research [found people clicked a link just 8% of the time when an AI summary appeared, versus 15%
without](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2025/07/22/google-users-are-less-likely-to-click-on-links-when-an-ai-summary-appears-in-the-results/).
Being the *cited* remodeler is the modern version of being the trusted renovation company in town.

## What actually decides who AI recommends?

Three things, in order — and they map onto exactly how a homeowner (and an answer engine) sizes up
a remodeler before trusting them with a five- or six-figure renovation.

That third gate is where most remodelers quietly lose. Ahrefs'
[study of 75,000 brands found off-site brand mentions correlate with AI visibility far more than
backlinks (0.664 vs 0.218)](/learn/mentions-vs-backlinks) — and for a remodeler, those mentions are
reviews, local press, supplier and association listings, and real project references. Earn them and
you become the trusted name; skip them and the platform keeps the spot it's reselling to you.
