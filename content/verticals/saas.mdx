## Why SaaS is an AEO opportunity

SaaS buyers research in public. They ask answer engines how to solve a problem
long before they evaluate vendors — which means the company whose content
_becomes the answer_ earns trust at the very top of the funnel.

## What works in this vertical

- **Documentation as content**: well-structured docs are some of the most
  citable pages a SaaS company owns.
- **Jobs-to-be-done explainers**: answer the question behind the search, not
  just the keyword.
- **Comparisons and integrations**: structured, honest, and answer-first.
