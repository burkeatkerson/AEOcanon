**Zero-click search is the answer with no website visit.** When a featured snippet,
AI Overview, or chatbot response satisfies the question in place, the user never
clicks a link — so the traditional payoff of ranking, the click, simply doesn't
happen. This pattern is why AEO exists.

It reframes the goal: when most queries end without a click, value shifts from getting
the visit to being the cited source inside the answer — the visibility, the brand
credit, and the one remaining path to your site. Measuring success therefore moves
from pageviews to [citation share](/glossary/citation-share) and the smaller but
high-intent [AI referral traffic](/glossary/ai-referral-traffic) that survives — the
[adaptability](/pillars/adaptability) pillar of tracking the right metric. Fighting
zero-click is futile; being the source it draws from is the play.

**Example.** Someone asks "what's the capital of Australia," reads "Canberra" in the
AI answer, and never clicks. The site that supplied that fact got cited, not visited —
which is the kind of win AEO now optimizes for.
