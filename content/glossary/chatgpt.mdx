**ChatGPT is OpenAI's AI assistant — and, with search turned on, a major answer
engine.** It answers in natural language, and when it uses search it retrieves live
web pages and cites them as sources, so being one of those cited pages is a direct
AEO goal.

Two different crawlers matter here. [GPTBot](/glossary/gptbot) gathers training
data, while [OAI-SearchBot](/glossary/oai-searchbot) is the one that fetches pages
for ChatGPT's live search citations — the bot you most want to allow. Once you're
crawlable, winning a ChatGPT citation comes down to being the most
[extractable](/pillars/extractability) answer and carrying the
[authority](/pillars/authority) that makes you a safe source to name.

**Example.** Ask ChatGPT "what's the best note-taking app for students" with search
on, and it returns a recommendation with linked sources beneath. AEO is the work of
making your page one of those linked sources, consistently, for your target
questions.
