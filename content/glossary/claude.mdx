**Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant.** It answers in natural language across a wide
range of tasks, and when given web access it can retrieve current pages and cite
them — acting as an answer engine whose responses can surface and credit your
content.

[ClaudeBot](/glossary/claudebot) is Anthropic's training crawler; user-initiated
web fetches use separate agents. For AEO the fundamentals hold regardless of engine:
serve crawlable HTML and write [extractable](/pillars/extractability), self-contained
answers, since that's what any retrieval-grounded assistant can lift and attribute.
Claude is also widely used through its API inside other products, so being citable
content benefits you across every tool built on it.

**Example.** A user asks Claude with web access to compare two project-management
tools; it fetches current pages and summarizes them with sources. A clear, evidenced
comparison page is exactly the kind of source that gets pulled into that answer.
