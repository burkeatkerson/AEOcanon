**People Also Ask shows the related questions real users ask.** It's the expandable
box of follow-up questions in Google results, each revealing a snippet answer. Beyond
its own visibility, it's a goldmine for AEO because it surfaces, for free, the actual
questions surrounding your topic.

That makes it a practical tool for the [alignment](/pillars/alignment) pillar: mine PAA
to find the real sub-questions people ask, then answer them with
[question-shaped headings](/glossary/question-shaped-heading) and clean, liftable
answers. It also previews how engines decompose topics — closely related to
[query fan-out](/glossary/query-fan-out) — so covering the PAA cluster around your
subject prepares you for both featured snippets and AI answers that draw on the same
sub-questions.

**Example.** Search "is sourdough healthy" and PAA might list "is sourdough good for
gut health," "does sourdough have less gluten," and "is sourdough lower glycemic."
Each is a sub-question to answer directly — and a chance to be cited.
