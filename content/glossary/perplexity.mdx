**Perplexity is an answer engine built around citing its sources.** It treats every
query as a search, retrieves relevant pages, and writes an answer with numbered
inline [citations](/glossary/citation) — making it unusually transparent about which
sources it used, and therefore a clear target for AEO.

Visibility in Perplexity depends first on being crawlable by
[PerplexityBot](/glossary/perplexitybot), then on being the passage its retrieval
step selects — the [extractability](/pillars/extractability) pillar — and on the
[authority](/pillars/authority) signals that decide which of several good sources
gets cited. Because its citations are so visible, Perplexity is a useful engine for
measuring whether your AEO work is landing.

**Example.** Ask Perplexity a question and the answer carries small numbered
references; hovering or clicking reveals the exact pages used. Tracking how often
your domain appears in those references, across your prompt set, is a direct read on
your Perplexity visibility.
