If your marketing data already lives in Semrush, its AI Visibility Toolkit is the
path of least resistance — and a capable one.

## What is the Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit?

It's Semrush's set of AI-visibility features, available within **Semrush One** (a
consolidated single subscription) and, for large organizations, via **Enterprise
AIO**. It tracks how your brand appears across the major answer engines and folds
that into the broader Semrush suite, so AI visibility sits alongside your keyword,
backlink, and site-audit data rather than in a separate tool.

## Key features

The toolkit spans **visibility overview, prompt research, competitor research,
brand performance, and an AI search site audit**. **Brand Performance** is the
centerpiece — it reports your **share of voice and sentiment** across Google AI
Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini, with reports updating
**weekly**. The AI site audit checks your pages against what engines reward, and
**Enterprise AIO** removes tracking limits and supports multiple markets and
brands for large teams. Sentiment analysis is a genuinely useful differentiator —
it's not just whether you're mentioned, but how.

## Pricing

Semrush keeps AI visibility inside its subscription model. As of June 2026, the
AI Visibility Toolkit is part of **Semrush One**, while **Enterprise AIO** is
**custom-quoted** (no public price) for large organizations needing scale and
fewer limits. The economics favor you if you already pay for Semrush; if you
don't, you're buying into a whole suite for the AI features. Confirm current
options at [semrush.com/pricing/ai](https://www.semrush.com/pricing/ai/).

## Who should use it?

Best for **existing Semrush customers** who want AI visibility in the same place
as their SEO data, and **enterprises** that need scale via AIO. If you're not in
the Semrush ecosystem, a focused daily tracker like [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review)
or [Otterly](/tools/otterly-review) is likely cheaper and faster to adopt.
