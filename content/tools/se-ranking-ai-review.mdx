SE Ranking's pitch is convenience: if your SEO already lives here, AI visibility
can too — without adding another vendor.

## What is SE Ranking's AI visibility tracking?

SE Ranking offers AI visibility three ways: the **full SEO suite**, a dedicated
standalone platform called **SE Visible**, and an **AI Search add-on** that bolts
AI tracking onto an existing SE Ranking subscription. All track how you appear in
AI answers — presence and ranking for your keywords, which domains are cited, and
historical trends — across six platforms including Google AI Overviews and AI
Mode. The appeal is integration: keywords, rankings, and AI visibility in one
familiar workflow.

## Key features

SE Ranking covers **six engines** (ChatGPT, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Google AI
Overviews, Google AI Mode, Gemini) with presence/ranking tracking, **source
analysis** showing which domains get cited, and **historical data with cached SERP
copies** so you can see how AI results shift over time and across geographies. For
SEO teams, the real value is having this beside the rest of their toolkit rather
than in a separate tab and separate invoice.

## Pricing

The standout is the **AI Search add-on at ~$89/month** for existing SE Ranking
customers — the cheapest way to add AI tracking if you're already in the suite.
The standalone **SE Visible** starts around **$189/month** (≈450 prompts), with
tiers at ~$355/month (1,000 prompts, 10 brands) and ~$519/month (1,500 prompts,
15 brands). The catch: SE Visible **refreshes weekly**, which feels slow next to
daily-sampling rivals at similar prices, and prompt caps fill quickly once you
track competitors across engines. Confirm current pricing at
[seranking.com](https://seranking.com).

## Who should use SE Ranking?

SE Ranking is the pragmatic choice for **SEO teams already using its suite** who
want AI visibility in the same place for ~$89/month, and for those who value
integrated historical SERP data. If you want daily sampling or you're not already
an SE Ranking customer, compare [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) and
[Otterly](/tools/otterly-review), which sample daily.

## Alternatives to SE Ranking
