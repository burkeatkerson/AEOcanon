Peec AI hits the sweet spot most marketing teams actually need: serious daily
tracking, clean reporting, and fair pricing — without enterprise overhead.

## What is Peec AI?

Peec AI is an AI search analytics platform that tracks how often your brand is
mentioned and cited across the major answer engines, built for **marketing teams
and agencies**. It shows your share of the conversations where your brand appears,
separates **mentions** (the answer names you) from **source citations** (your
content is used, even if you're not named), and lets you manage multiple brands or
sub-brands under one account.

## Key features

The standout is fit-for-purpose simplicity at a fair price. Peec tracks **six
engines** — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, Google AI Mode, and AI
Overviews — and runs your prompts **once every 24 hours** for dependable trend
data. Every plan includes **unlimited users**, which is unusual and genuinely
useful for teams and agencies, plus centralized billing and flexible prompt
allocation across client projects. The mentions-vs-citations split is clearer than
some rivals', which helps you see whether you're being named, merely used as a
source, or neither.

## Pricing

Peec AI is transparently priced for the mid-market. As of June 2026, the
**Starter plan is about $95/month** (≈50 prompts, three models, unlimited users,
daily tracking, one project), with a **15% discount for annual billing**, and
higher tiers add prompts and models at a lower per-prompt rate. Because pricing
scales by prompts × models, wide tracking across many questions and engines adds
up — size your plan to the prompts you'll actually use. Confirm current pricing at
[peec.ai](https://peec.ai).

## Who should use Peec AI?

Peec AI is ideal for **in-house marketing teams and agencies** that want reliable
daily AI visibility tracking with clean reporting and multi-brand support, without
paying enterprise rates. Solo users on a tight budget may prefer
[Otterly's](/tools/otterly-review) lower entry tier; enterprises needing 10+
engines and built-in content workflows will lean to
[Profound](/tools/profound-review).
