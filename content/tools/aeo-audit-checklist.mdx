**This is a free, interactive AEO audit checklist scored against the eight AEO
Canon pillars.** Tick every item that's already true of your site, read your score
per pillar, and fix the earliest unchecked layer first — because the pillars
cascade, an unaddressed item near the top outranks anything below it.

## How to read your score

Your total is how much of the citable-site checklist you've done; your per-pillar
scores show *where* the gaps are. Don't optimize evenly — the pillars are ordered
on purpose. If [Access](/pillars/access) or [Extractability](/pillars/extractability)
has unchecked items, fix those before touching [Freshness](/pillars/freshness) or
[Adaptability](/pillars/adaptability), because an engine that can't read or
[lift](/glossary/passage-retrieval) your page never gets to the rest. This is the same logic as [using the Canon as a
diagnostic](/learn/aeo-canon-diagnostic): find the first broken gate and start
there.
