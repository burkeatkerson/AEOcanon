## What can you get for free?

For free, you can get two of the three things an AEO program needs: a baseline
audit and ongoing visibility tracking. (The third — actually improving your
content and authority — is work, not software.) A free **audit** scores how
AI-ready your pages are; a free **tracker** runs a small [prompt
set](/glossary/prompt-set) and reports who gets [cited](/glossary/citation). Together they're enough to start. For the manual method that
needs no tools at all, see [how to measure your AI visibility](/measure-aeo).

## The bottom line

A free audit plus a free tracker is enough to run a serious AEO program for the
first 90 days — and the work that actually moves your visibility (answer-first
content, evidence, and authority) is free anyway. When you outgrow free, compare
your options in [the best AI visibility tools](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools),
and ground the strategy in [what is AEO](/learn/what-is-aeo) and
[The AEO Canon](/learn/aeo-canon).
