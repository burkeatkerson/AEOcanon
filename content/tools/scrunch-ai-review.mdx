Scrunch AI is one of the few tools that goes past measurement into actively
shaping what AI crawlers receive.

## What is Scrunch AI?

Scrunch AI is a brand monitoring and optimization platform for the AI era. It does
the expected job — showing how your brand appears across ChatGPT, Gemini,
Perplexity, and other generative platforms, which content LLMs pull from, and how
competitors stack up — but adds two things: **Insights** that explain *why* AI
represents you the way it does and what to do about it, and an **Agent Experience
Platform (AXP)** that serves AI crawlers structured, LLM-optimized content.

## Key features

Beyond real-time monitoring and competitive visibility, Scrunch's differentiator
is **AXP** — an infrastructure layer between your site and AI crawlers that
delivers compressed, structured content tuned for LLM consumption without
disrupting the human experience. That's a meaningfully different bet from
pure-monitoring tools: it tries to influence what the machine ingests, not just
report what it produced. The **Insights** layer is also more action-oriented than
most, translating findings into recommended moves rather than leaving you with
dashboards.

## Pricing

Scrunch is a mid-market-to-enterprise tool, priced accordingly. As of June 2026,
entry pricing is reported around **$250/month** (some sources cite ~$300 for basic
functionality), scaling to **custom enterprise**, with a **7-day free trial** that
helps given the cost. Confirm current pricing at [scrunch.com](https://scrunch.com).

## Who should use Scrunch AI?

Scrunch fits **midsize enterprises with a dedicated AEO team** and **agencies
serving a few high-value clients** — organizations that can both afford it and act
on its insights. It's explicitly not for solo marketers, freelancers, or small
businesses without an SEO/AEO owner; for those, start with
[Otterly](/tools/otterly-review) or [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review).

## Alternatives to Scrunch AI

The closest peer is [Profound](/tools/profound-review) (broader engines + content
generation). For accessible tracking, see [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) and
[Otterly](/tools/otterly-review); for suite add-ons,
[Ahrefs Brand Radar](/tools/ahrefs-brand-radar-review) and
[Semrush AI Visibility](/tools/semrush-ai-visibility-review). Compare them in
[the best AI visibility tools](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools); the crawler-facing
content idea connects to [the Access pillar](/learn/aeo-pillar-access).
