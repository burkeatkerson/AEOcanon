Otterly.AI is where most teams should start: a real entry price, and more than a
bare mention tracker once you're in.

## What is Otterly.AI?

Otterly.AI is an AI search monitoring tool that tracks how your brand shows up
across the major answer engines and helps you act on it. It's pitched at the
accessible end of the market, with a genuine low-cost tier, but it includes more
than mention counting — keyword-to-prompt research, daily monitoring, a brand
visibility index, link-citation analysis, and a **GEO audit** that turns results
into a prioritized plan.

## Key features

Otterly covers **six engines** (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google
AI Mode, Gemini, Copilot) and checks your chosen prompts **daily**, storing the
answers so you can see how they change. Its **AI keyword research** turns your
keywords, brand, or URL into the real prompts people use, which is a smart way to
build a prompt set if you're starting from scratch. The **GEO audit** — SWOT, a
competitor set, and tactic gaps — is the feature that most distinguishes it from a
plain tracker, because it points you at what to fix, not just what's wrong.

## Pricing

Otterly is the most accessible serious tracker. As of June 2026, tiers are
prompt-based: **Lite from ~$29/month** (~10 prompts), **Standard ~$189/month**
(100 prompts), and **Pro ~$989/month** (1,000 prompts), with a free trial. The
entry price is excellent; just note the per-prompt model scales steeply, so heavy
multi-engine tracking lands you in the upper tiers fast. Confirm current pricing
at [otterly.ai](https://otterly.ai).

## Who should use Otterly.AI?

Otterly is the best choice for **SMBs, solo marketers, and anyone starting AEO
measurement** — the low entry price plus the GEO audit make it easy to begin and
act. Teams that need unlimited users and multi-brand reporting should weigh
[Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review); enterprises needing 10+ engines and content
workflows will prefer [Profound](/tools/profound-review).

## Alternatives to Otterly.AI

The closest accessible alternative is [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) (more
team/agency features, unlimited users); at the enterprise end, see
[Profound](/tools/profound-review) and [Scrunch](/tools/scrunch-ai-review). For
free options, see [the best free AEO tools](/tools/best-free-aeo-tools), and the
full lineup in [the best AI visibility tools](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools).
