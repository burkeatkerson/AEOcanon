Brand Radar is Ahrefs' answer to AI visibility — and like Semrush's, its best
argument is that your SEO data already lives there.

## What is Ahrefs Brand Radar?

Brand Radar is an add-on inside the Ahrefs ecosystem that tracks how brands,
products, and entities appear across **AI answers and the open web**. Backed by a
database of **250M+ prompts**, it shows share of voice over time, which domains are
cited most in AI answers, and — usefully — lets you **cross-reference AI mentions
with branded search demand and web/social mentions**, tying AI visibility back to
real interest in your brand.

## Key features

Brand Radar covers **six engines** (Google AI Overviews, AI Mode, ChatGPT,
Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot) and uniquely connects AI visibility to the rest of
the Ahrefs picture: branded search demand, web mentions, and an add-on module that
**scans YouTube transcripts, TikTok captions, and Reddit threads** for brand
mentions — relevant given those are among the most-cited sources in AI answers. A
**free tier** lets you watch custom prompts and see competitor overlap, which is a
genuinely useful no-cost baseline.

## Pricing

This is where Brand Radar gets expensive. It **requires an Ahrefs subscription**
(Lite from ~$129/month), then the **AI indexes are sold per platform at ~$199/month
each**, or **bundled across six for ~$699/month**, with the social module another
~$199/month. Independent reviews put typical all-in cost at **~$828–$1,148/month**.
Confirm current pricing at [ahrefs.com](https://ahrefs.com).

## Limitations to know

Two matter. First, despite covering six engines, Brand Radar **does not track
Claude or Grok** — a notable omission at this price. Second, user reviews from
late 2025 and early 2026 consistently note that the **ChatGPT and Perplexity
modules can underreport** mentions. Spot-check against a manual run (see
[how to measure your AI visibility](/measure-aeo)) before trusting the numbers
for decisions.

## Who should use it?

Best for **existing Ahrefs users** who want AI mentions cross-referenced with
their SEO and branded-search data, and who value the social-mention scanning. If
you're not already on Ahrefs, the all-in cost is hard to justify versus a focused
daily tracker like [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) or
[Otterly](/tools/otterly-review).
