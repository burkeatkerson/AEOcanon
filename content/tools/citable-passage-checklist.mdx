## What the score can and can't tell you

This is a heuristic, so treat it as a spell-checker for extractability, not a
verdict from a real engine. It catches the mechanical reasons a passage is hard to
quote — a filler opener, an orphan "this/it" reference, missing evidence, run-on
sentences — but it can't tell whether your answer is *correct* or genuinely
[original](/pillars/originality). Pass the checks, then make sure the substance is
worth citing.
