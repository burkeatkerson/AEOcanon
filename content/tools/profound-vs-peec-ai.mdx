## Where Profound wins

Profound wins on **breadth and execution**. It tracks 10+ engines — including
[Claude](/glossary/claude), Grok, DeepSeek, and Meta AI that Peec doesn't — and its **Agents**
workflow turns insights into AEO content and publishes to a CMS, so measurement
and action live in one platform. It's also the best-funded, most
enterprise-proven option, which matters if you're standardizing a program across
many brands or markets. Read the full [Profound review](/tools/profound-review).

## Where Peec AI wins

Peec wins on **value and team fit**. Transparent pricing from ~$95/month,
**unlimited users on every plan**, daily tracking across the six engines most
audiences actually use, and clean multi-brand/agency reporting cover the core job
without enterprise overhead — at a fraction of Profound's cost. For the large
majority of marketing teams, that's the right trade. Read the full
[Peec AI review](/tools/peec-ai-review).

## Which should you choose?

Choose **Peec AI** unless you have a specific reason to pay for Profound. That
reason is real for enterprises: you need Claude/Grok coverage, a built-in content
workflow, or scale across many brands and markets. Absent those, Peec gives most
teams everything they need. Still deciding? Compare the whole field in
[the best AI visibility tools](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools), or weigh the
cheaper entry option in [Otterly vs Peec AI](/tools/otterly-vs-peec-ai). Whatever
you pick, the tool only measures — winning [citations](/glossary/citation) is
[The AEO Canon](/learn/aeo-canon).
