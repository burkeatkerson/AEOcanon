  - q: What is the best alternative to Profound?

  - q: Why would I switch from Profound?
    a: Usually cost. Profound is the most comprehensive platform but is priced for enterprises, which is overkill for small and mid-market teams that mainly need to track how engines cite them. Teams also switch to consolidate AI tracking into an SEO suite they already use (Ahrefs or Semrush).
  - q: Is there a cheaper tool that does what Profound does?
    a: For core tracking, yes — Peec AI and Otterly cover how engines cite you across the main platforms for far less. What you give up is Profound's breadth (10+ engines) and its built-in content-generation workflow. If you don't need those, the cheaper tools are enough.

**Scrunch**, or consolidate into **Ahrefs** or **Semrush** if you already use
