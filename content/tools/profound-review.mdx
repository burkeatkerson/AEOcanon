Profound is the enterprise standard for AI visibility — the most comprehensive
platform in the category, and the one large brands reach for first.

## What is Profound?

Profound is an AI visibility and answer-engine-optimization platform that tracks
how your brand appears across the major AI answer engines and helps you act on
what it finds. Its core **Answer Engine Insights** show how often you're
mentioned, how competitors appear, and which sources each engine cites — captured
directly from the browser, so what you see is close to what a real user sees. In
late 2025 it added **Agents**, a content workflow that generates and refreshes
AEO content (Content Refresh, AEO FAQ generation, competitive research) and
publishes to a CMS — a direct answer to the "monitoring-only" critique of early
trackers.

## Key features

Profound's standout is breadth and depth. It covers **10+ engines** — ChatGPT,
Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, DeepSeek, Grok, Meta AI, and Google AI
Overviews — which is the widest coverage among the tools we track. Beyond
tracking, the Agents workflow (drag-and-drop builder, templates, brand-kit
injection, knowledge-base retrieval, CMS publishing) moves it from measurement
into execution. It's also the best-funded player in the space: a **$96M Series C
in early 2026 at a ~$1B valuation**, with enterprise customers including Ramp,
DocuSign, Figma, and large retailers — signals of staying power that matter when
you're standardizing a program on one vendor.

## Pricing

Profound is priced for the enterprise. As of June 2026, reported entry tiers run
roughly **$80–$400/month** (a Starter plan tracking ChatGPT with around 50
prompts; a Growth plan spanning three engines), while the full platform is
**custom/enterprise**. Pricing and tier definitions shift frequently and vary by
source, so confirm current numbers on [tryprofound.com](https://www.tryprofound.com).
The practical read: the real value — full engine coverage plus Agents —
concentrates at higher spend.

## Who should use Profound?

Profound is the right call for **enterprises and well-funded teams** running AEO
as a serious program across many engines, brands, or markets — especially those
who want measurement and content execution in one platform. If you're a small
business, solo marketer, or just starting out, it's more than you need; you'll get
the core measurement from [Otterly](/tools/otterly-review) or
[Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) for far less, and can graduate to Profound when
scale demands it.

## Alternatives to Profound

The closest enterprise alternative is [Scrunch AI](/tools/scrunch-ai-review),
which adds an Agent Experience Platform that serves structured content to AI
crawlers. For accessible measurement, see [Peec AI](/tools/peec-ai-review) and
[Otterly](/tools/otterly-review); if you already pay for a suite,
[Ahrefs Brand Radar](/tools/ahrefs-brand-radar-review) or
[Semrush's AI Visibility Toolkit](/tools/semrush-ai-visibility-review) may cover
your needs. Compare them all in

tool only measures — winning citations is the work of
[The AEO Canon](/learn/aeo-canon). New to measurement? Start with
