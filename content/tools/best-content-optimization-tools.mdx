## What "content optimization for AEO" really means

Content optimization for AEO means shaping content so an engine can extract and
trust it — answer-first passages, inline evidence, question-shaped headings, and
genuine originality. That's different from traditional SEO content tools focused on
keyword density, and it's why the controlled research matters: the
[Princeton GEO study (arXiv 2311.09735)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09735) found
that adding citations, quotations, and statistics lifted AI visibility, while
keyword stuffing did not. Good tools accelerate that substance; they can't
substitute for it. The principles live in
[the Extractability](/pillars/extractability) and
[Credibility](/pillars/credibility) pillars.

## How to actually use them

Use content tools in a loop with a visibility tracker: measure, fix, re-measure.
Run a [visibility tracker](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools) to find the questions
where competitors are cited and you aren't; use research and audit tools to
understand the gap; rewrite the passage answer-first with inline evidence (a scorer
helps here); then re-check the tracker to confirm movement. The tools make the loop
faster — the substance you add is what closes it.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Don't outsource the substance">
