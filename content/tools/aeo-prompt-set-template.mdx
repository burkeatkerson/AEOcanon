## What makes a good tracking prompt

A good prompt is a real, conversational question a customer would actually ask an
AI on the way to buying — specific and intent-rich. "Best CRM for a 10-person
agency" beats "CRM." Weight your set toward decision-stage questions, where being
cited has the most commercial value, and keep it fixed once it's good, because
comparability over time is the whole point of tracking. This is the [Alignment
pillar](/pillars/alignment) applied to measurement.
