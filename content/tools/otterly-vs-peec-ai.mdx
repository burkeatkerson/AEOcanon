## Where Otterly wins

Otterly wins on **price and getting started**. Its ~$29/month entry is the lowest
serious on-ramp in the category, and the built-in **[GEO](/glossary/generative-engine-optimization)
audit** (SWOT, competitor set, tactic gaps) plus AI keyword research help you build a prompt set and act on
results — not just watch a dashboard. The caveat is the per-prompt model, which
gets expensive at volume. Full [Otterly review](/tools/otterly-review).

## Where Peec AI wins

Peec wins on **team and agency fit**. Unlimited users on every plan, clean
multi-brand reporting, and centralized billing make it the better choice once more
than one person needs access or you're managing several clients — worth the higher
entry price for teams. Full [Peec AI review](/tools/peec-ai-review).

## Which should you choose?

Start with **Otterly** if you're solo, budget-conscious, or just validating AEO;
move to **Peec AI** when you have a team or clients and need unlimited users and
multi-brand reporting. Many brands begin on Otterly and graduate to Peec (or an
enterprise tool) as the program grows. Compare everything in
[the best AI visibility tools](/tools/best-ai-visibility-tools), and for the
enterprise tier see [Profound vs Peec AI](/tools/profound-vs-peec-ai). Then put
the data to work with [The AEO Canon](/learn/aeo-canon).
