**YouTube mentions are the single strongest correlate of AI visibility, so
genuinely useful video — and being mentioned in others' videos — is one of the
highest-impact off-site authority moves you can make.** As with every surface,
the win comes from real value, not gaming.

## Platform overview

YouTube is the world's second-largest search engine and a primary source of
how-to, review, and explainer content — exactly the experience-based material
answer engines favor. Both your own videos and mentions of your brand in others'
videos contribute to the signal, and YouTube pages are themselves frequently cited
in AI answers.

## Why answer engines trust YouTube

Answer engines trust YouTube because video content — especially demonstrations,
reviews, and expert explanations — is hard to fake and rich in real experience.

[Ahrefs' analysis of 75,000 brands](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-brand-visibility-correlations/)
found YouTube mentions to be the strongest correlate of AI visibility of any
factor tested — ahead of general brand mentions and far ahead of backlinks. That
makes video a core part of the [Authority pillar](/learn/aeo-pillar-authority).

## The genuine-presence playbook

Win on YouTube by making — and earning a place in — videos that genuinely help
people, then making them easy for engines to read.

## What NOT to do

<Callout variant="warning" title="No fake engagement, no keyword-stuffed spam">
  <p>
    Don't buy views or subscribers, stuff keywords into titles and descriptions,
    or pump out thin, AI-spun videos with no real value. Inflated metrics don't
    create the genuine signal engines reward, and low-value uploads can damage
    trust in your channel. The Canon rejects manipulation because engines are
    built to find genuine usefulness — so be genuinely useful.
  </p>
</Callout>

## How to measure it

Measure YouTube's contribution by tracking mentions and citations, not raw view
counts. Watch for: your videos or videos mentioning you appearing in AI answers;
growth in branded search after features; referral traffic from youtube.com; and
the number of credible channels that mention your brand or experts. Pair this with
the broader [branded mentions](/authority/build-branded-mentions) effort, and use
the [Off-Site Authority Audit](/authority/off-site-authority-audit) to see where
video fits among your other gaps.
