**Branded mentions — your name appearing across the web, linked or not — are the
strongest controllable correlate of AI visibility, so the strategy is to be
genuinely worth mentioning everywhere your audience and the engines look.** This is
the synthesis playbook: it ties every channel together into one coherent,
recognizable presence.

## Platform overview

Branded mentions aren't a single platform — they're the aggregate of how often and
how consistently the web references your brand and experts, across every surface in
this cluster. Engines assemble their understanding of you from this distributed
signal: the more places that talk about you accurately, the more defined and
trusted your entity becomes. This playbook is the connective tissue across
[Reddit](/authority/reddit-aeo-playbook),
[YouTube](/authority/youtube-aeo-playbook),
[publications](/authority/get-mentioned-in-publications),
[reviews](/authority/review-platform-playbook), and the rest.

## Why answer engines weight mentions

Answer engines weight branded mentions because they're the web's distributed,
hard-to-fake verdict on who matters in a topic.

Two findings define the strategy. [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-overview-brand-correlation/)
shows mentions are the dominant off-site correlate of AI visibility. And
[Semrush's volatility research](https://www.semrush.com/blog/url-volatility-ai-overviews/)
shows citations are unstable — Google keeps a given URL in an AI Overview only
about **3.87 days** on average, and 40–60% of cited sources change month to month.
Together they make the case: don't chase one citation; build a broad, durable base
of mentions that keeps you present through the churn. That's the
[Authority pillar](/learn/aeo-pillar-authority) meeting the
[Adaptability pillar](/learn/aeo-pillar-adaptability).

## What NOT to do

<Callout variant="warning" title="No fake mentions, mention networks, or astroturfing">
  <p>
    Don't manufacture mentions through PBNs, paid mention networks, sockpuppets, or
    astroturfed "buzz." Engines and platforms increasingly detect coordinated
    inauthentic activity, and a manufactured footprint is both fragile and a
    reputational liability. The Canon rejects gaming because the signal engines
    reward is genuine, distributed trust — which by definition can't be faked into
    existence. Earn the mentions.
  </p>
</Callout>

## How to measure it

Measure branded mentions by volume, breadth, sentiment, and resilience. Track:
total brand mentions across sources over time (linked and unlinked); the diversity
of platforms mentioning you; sentiment and accuracy of how you're described; and
whether your AI visibility holds steady through citation churn rather than spiking
and collapsing. Branded search volume is a strong proxy — when the web talks about
you more, more people search your name directly.
